# 7 Great Websites to Score Free Samples on Your Favorite Products ...

If you've never looked for websites to score free samples, you should consider it. Most people are amazed when they find out that they can get tons of free stuff from product manufacturers. I have been able to snag great things like coupons for free baby formula, laundry detergent samples, and toothpaste just to name a few. If you get a chance to check them out, here are 7 websites to score free samples, that I currently use. After all, free samples are a great way to try a product before you buy it and many products come with coupons.

## 1. PG Brand Sampler

![PG Brand Sampler](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kg/bn/vq/xxya49el_400x300.jpg)

One of my favorite websites to score free samples is PG Brand Sampler. Many people are surprised when they learn that Proctor and Gamble actually manufacturers a number of great products. When I signed up for their free samples, they mailed me a packet that had samples of Tide laundry detergent, Downy Unstopables, Crest toothpaste, and Crest Whitestrips. Plus, they sent me an entire booklet of coupons. Be sure to check back with time to time because new samples are added frequently.

## 2. Walmart

![Walmart](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x3/lx/n1/jk28it60_600x411.jpg)

Walmart offers a variety of different samples in their stores. Usually these samples come with a coupon that makes that particular item free or nearly free. You can also check out Walmart online to get free samples and coupons. Most of their samples are for items that people use on a regular basis, like deodorant or toothpaste. Be sure to snag samples quickly because they tend to go quickly.

## 3. Sample Buddy

![Sample Buddy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dl/sg/zx/i38ei16x_400x300.jpg)

With food cost on the rise, it's always good to know that you will like the foods you're buying. One great way to do this is by trying food samples. This can help you find a new food for your family to try and also prevent you from buying an item that won't be eaten. Sample Buddy has a ton of different food food samples that you can get. I have been able to get free samples of cereal, coffee, and candy.

## 4. Gerber

![Gerber](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0h/xj/yn/0i2pklwp_600x368.jpg)

Gerber was one of my favorite places for samples when I was pregnant. In order to get free samples from Gerber, you now have to print off a certificate from their website to take with you to your doctors office. If you plan to formula feed, it's definitely worth it. Until my son was about 18 months, they sent me coupons for formula. Some of the coupons were $2 or $3 but some of them were for $11.

## 5. Start Sampling

![Start Sampling](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ly/m7/xr/gbcecyms_400x300.jpg)

If you're looking for an easy to understand sample site, check out Start Sampling. Each sample that's currently available is listed with both a picture and a link to the manufacturer. My favorite thing about this one is that you can read product reviews. If the item is something that you've tried, you can also leave a review for others to read. Start Sampling also has a ton of different coupons that you can print off, too.

## 6. Similac

![Similac](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f5/d0/0h/omduxxj8_400x300.jpg)

This is another great site for Moms-To-Be to check out. You can register for their Similac Strong Moms membership and receive lots of free stuff throughout your pregnancy and even after baby arrives. As part of your membership you will receive tons of free samples of different baby products. You will also receive free formula, breast milk storage containers, vitamins, and some great formula coupons.

## 7. Scam Free Samples

_ It's always important to watch out for scams, even when getting samples. You should always remember that free samples should be free. I like to visit Scam Free Samples because they work hard to make sure that all of the samples listed on their website are the real deal. Their website even offers additional tips on how to avoid scams. If you like, you can also sign up for email updates, which will alert you as soon as new samples are added.
