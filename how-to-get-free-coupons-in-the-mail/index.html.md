# How to Get Free Coupons in the Mail ...

The YouTube video may be a little drawn out, but still worth watching. In the video, learn **how to receive coupons in the mail**!

All you need to do is go to your favorite product's website, go to the contact option and write a little compliment, your name, and address. Do not just type that you want a coupon.

Have a baby that only likes Gerber? Got a picky pet? Like an expensive make-up brand? Prefer a certain brand of coffee? Whatever you like, you could be getting coupons for any product at almost half off or even free.

I hope you have found this video to be inspiring and excited to save money... and not to mention spoiled with products that you love.

Good luck you frugal gal!
