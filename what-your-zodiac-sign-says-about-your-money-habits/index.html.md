# What Your Zodiac Sign Says about Your Money Habits ...

They say that money makes the world go round, but that can only be true for you personally if you have any! We are all different as human beings, and we certainly have our own different ways of handling our finances; some sensible, some not so sensible! If you want to start making changes in your life, you first need to have an understating of your true financial nature. Here is some information about **your money habits according to your zodiac sign**.

## 1. Aries

You are known for your risk-taking, so saving up isn’t something that you are inclined to do! The likelihood is that you are going to continue with your fast living, care free financial ways in 2019.

## 2. Taurus

There is nothing you love more than stability. You like to treat yourself every now and then, but for the main part, you are great at saving what needs to be saved and not going over the top with your purchases.

## 3. Gemini

You are really dynamic and smart when it comes to money. You know exactly how much you have, and you are always coming up with clever ways to spend it that gets you more for your money.

## 4. Cancer

You are very emotionally lead when it comes to money. You always listen to your heart when sometimes you really should be listening to your head instead. It runs the risk of getting you in trouble!

## 5. Leo

You are extremely good with your money management because you also have a great knack of being able to make lots of it! Your out-there personality means that you are always first in mind for things like promotions.

## 6. Virgo

You have never been one to spend irrationally. You are too practical to buy things that you know you are never going to use, and you are extremely good at resisting all kinds of retail temptation.

## 7. Libra

You have a really tough time making decisions, which in finance can be a good thing because it prevents you from being an impulse buyer. You end up saving more than you spend.

## 8. Scorpio

You are the kind of person who is always looking to turn one dollar into two, so you have more than a few irons in the fire when it comes to finance and investments.

## 9. Sagittarius

A classic Sagittarius lives her life as YOLO as you can! Any savings that you have, you spend on things like travel to get as many experiences and memories under your belt as you can.

## 10. Capricorn

Your main focus in life is climbing the career ladder, so you are prepared to spend money on things that will help you get to the top, but you don’t tend to be frivolous in other areas.

## 11. Aquarius

You are probably the best balancer of the books out of all the signs! Your accounts are a dream, you have the right amount saved, and the right amount to spend each month, it’s no big deal for you!

## 12. Pisces

Once you have paid the rent or the mortgage, you feel like your money is yours to spend on whatever you want. It might not be the best plan for the future, but hey, you’re living here today, right?
