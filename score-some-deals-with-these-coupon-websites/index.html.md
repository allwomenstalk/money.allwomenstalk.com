# Score Some Deals with These Coupon Websites ...

I love to save money. It’s a great feeling that you’ve got one over on the retailer when you grab a bargain. You can save by comparing prices online with those in store, you can use money saving apps, or you can use coupons. I know it can be a chore clipping coupons, but here are websites that have made couponing fun and easy.

## 1. RetailMeNot

This site is definitely a dream come true for a real coupon lover. With over 500,000 coupons from more than 50,000 stores, you can find a deal for pretty much everything. And, they have an app available to help you score discounts from your smartphone while you're on the move.
Site - _retailmenot.com_

## 2. Shop at Home

Shop at Home has 94 million proud members who have subscribed for their Cash Back Rewards program. No longer do you need to pay all that moolah to get your favorite pair of jeans or your favorite handbag – you can get it for less by getting 30%, and sometimes more, cash back on every dollar spent.
Site - [shopathome.com](https://www.tada.com/)

## 3. The Krazy Coupon Lady

The internet is a great way of sharing what you know, and that's exactly what two friends are doing at The Krazy Coupon Lady. They used to exchange great savings and deals with each other, but they then decided to turn it into a website and let others enjoy those great discounts as well. Today, it's one of the very best coupon websites with discounts in almost every category.
Site - [thekrazycouponlady.com](https://thekrazycouponlady.com/)

## 4. Coupons

You gotta love websites with names that say exactly what’s in the tin. Some sites have so much going on it can be overwhelming. You spend so much time navigating that it isn’t worth the savings you eventually find. That’s why you’ll love the simplistic way this site is laid out.
Site - [coupons.com](http://www.coupons.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Woot

You will find a new deal every day, and that's what makes it special. The website is clean with an easy-to-use navigation structure. It is super simple to score affordable home goods, shoes, tech, and much more. Go check "Today's Woot!" now!
Site - [home.woot.com](http://home.woot.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. Slickdeals

Yes, it has become rather simple to find deals online. The question is, "do all of them really deserve your attention?" Wouldn't that be great if you could read what others say about the deals they may have used already? That's where Slickdeals excels greatly. It shares a rating score from past users – you will also know how many people actually want that deal to feature again. Try it to make a sensible buying decision!
Site - [slickdeals.net](https://slickdeals.net/)

## 7. Free Shipping

How many times have you been frustrated by shipping costs? You find a bargain, but the P&P pushes the cost over what you’d pay if you went to the mall and purchased in store. At Freeshipping.com, you can shop to your heart's content and never worry about the shipping cost. It saves you from searching high and low to find those elusive promo codes to save on the shipping cost. Try it – it's really worth a visit!
Site - [freeshipping.org](https://goo.gl/06xrzK)

## 8. Groupon

It may be one of the oldest coupon/money saving sites, but that means it leverages its experience to bring you some great deals. I like Groupon for anything that isn’t of an everyday nature. If you want to treat yourself, look on Groupon before parting with cash.
Site - [groupon.com](http://www.groupon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 9. Mambo Sprouts

This may seem a little left field compared to the others. I agree it is a niche site, but so many of us wish we could have a more organic lifestyle and are put off by prohibitive costs. If you want to eat more organic food and introduce more organic products to your life, check out the deals here.
Site - [mambosprouts.com](http://www.mambosprouts.com/)

This is just one of the straws in a haystack of coupon sites out there. Why not tell us your favorite so we have a great library of deal sites?
