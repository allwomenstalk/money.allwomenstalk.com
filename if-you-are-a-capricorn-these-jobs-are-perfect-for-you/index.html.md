# If You Are a Capricorn These Jobs Are Perfect for You ...

Capricorn loves the stability of a steady job that other signs like Sagittarius would hate. This sign is more than happy to work in a job with a regular pay check and fixed office space, and need to be surrounded by structure and discipline. But as well as liking structure in their own lives, they're also great at organizing others. So if you're a Capricorn, these careers can be perfect for you …

## 1. Computer Programmer

Being a computer programmer means long hours spent poring over code, and that's something organized Capricorn loves. When there's a problem with a web page or program, this sign will happily glue themselves to the screen until they've got it all sorted. Dealing with the data that would drive other signs crazy is all in a day's work for them.

## 2. Financial Advisor

Capricorn is a financially responsible sign, whether they're dealing with their own money or someone else's. So they're well suited to being a financial advisor, bank clerk or accountant. They'll pay close attention to making all the sums add up, and will be scrupulously honest when looking after other people's money. The conservative nature of the financial world is something they're very comfortable with.

## 3. Organizer

The Goat's organizational abilities mean that they can not only get figures in order, but things as well. So if you're a Capricorn, you could turn this to your advantage and set up a business as a home organizer. For this sign, there is a place for everything, and everything in its place; you'll have every home in town neat and tidy before the owners can even blink.

## 4. Accountant

Accountants are often unfairly characterized as being boring, but Capricorn isn't worried if people think this about them. It's a career that they're made for, and since there will always be a need for accountants Capricorn will appreciate the steady pay check that comes with this career. Responsibility is the hallmark of this sign, and accountancy allows them to take care of themselves as much as of other people.

## 5. Real Estate

As an earth sign, Capricorn does well with any career connected to the home or building. They can flourish as a real estate developer, work in construction, or sell homes to buyers. Capricorn appreciates the importance of being in the right home, so they will work hard to find you the home that you can be happy in.

## 6. Dentistry

The teeth are actually associated with Capricorn, and since dentistry is a very steady profession it's a doubly appropriate career for this sign. There will always be a demand for dentists, so Capricorn will be assured of a constant stream of clients. As well as going to dental school, the Goat could become a dental nurse, hygienist, or even a dental surgeon.

## 7. Corporations

You don't get to the top of the corporate world without being disciplined and determined, and many of the top businessmen and women are actually Capricorns. Jeff Bezos of Amazon and Christine Lagarde, head of the International Monetary Fund, are among them - and there are few people more significant in the business and financial world at the moment. Capricorns are hard workers, and can handle the commitment and dedication that working in the corporate world demands.
