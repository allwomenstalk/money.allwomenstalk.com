# 7 Bargain Shopping Sites to save Big on Fashion and Beauty Products ...

I love a good deal, and if you’re a self-professed shopping addict like me, you know that bargain shopping sites are the holy grail of good deals! Part of online shopping is knowing where to look for the best bargains, and it’s a piece of cake from there. With the holidays coming around, it's important that I find something for everyone on my list - and buy myself a little something too - without breaking the bank. That’s where bargain shopping sites come in!

## 1. Hautelook.com

Hautelook.com is my personal favorite of all the bargain shopping sites. Hautelook offers up to 75% off beauty, both men and women’s fashion and home decor as well. It features what they call Flash Sales where they have daily deals that are a limited time only. With brands such as NYX Cosmetics, Calvin Klein and Betsey Johnson, Hautelook easily has something for everyone! And to top it all off, they offer free shipping for any order over $100!

## 2. Shopittome.com

Shopittome.com offers anywhere between 40 to 70% off brand name designers and the best part about it is that the site is totally tailored to you. When you first register, you answer a series of questions such as your clothing and shoe size, and your favorite designers so that every time you use the site it's personalized for you and your own taste! Shopittome.com shows you everything you love and none of what you don’t!

## 3. AllCosmeticsWholesale.com

Not many people know about this little beauty gem! AllCosmeticsWholesale.com has your favorite beauty brands at super discounted prices. NYX, Laura Mercier, Korres, Lorac, and Urban Decay products plus so many more can be found here. I once bought a Bobbi Brown eye palette that had a retail value of $55 for $30... now that’s what I call a bargain. The best part about All Cosmetics Wholesale is that they even feature discontinued products. So that favorite lipstick that you owned and cherished before and were disappointed when you found out it was discontinued, or you couldn’t find it anywhere, can be found on allcosmeticswholesale.com.

## 4. Us.Strawberrynet.com

Strawberrynet.com is a discounted makeup and beauty site that carries high end and drugstore brand makeup at dirt cheap prices. Its perfect for finding that one item you wanted but didn’t want to splurge on when it first came out. For months, I sought an Urban Decay eyeshadow but didn’t want to shell out 20 bucks for a single eye shadow. I later found that same exact eye shadow on Strawberrynet.com for much less and I was super pleased with my purchase! Oh, and did I mention, skincare and makeup items from Strawberrynet.com ship free to anywhere in the world, no coupon code required!

## 5. Modnique.com

Similar to Hautelook.com, Modnique holds flash sales featuring designer brands in beauty and fashion. With discounts up to 85%, Modnique is definitely a favorite site of mine, especially during the holidays! They even have a children’s section filled with adorable clothes and accessories, and a men’s section as well. With new sales arriving every day, you’re sure to find something you like.

## 6. Ruelala.com

Ruelala is a member’s only site similar to that of Hautelook.com. Everyday at 11AM Eastern time they open up new boutiques that are only available for just 48 hours. In that time, you’ll be able to browse hundreds of designer clothing items, shoes, home decor and much more at unbelievable markdowns! To become a part of this member’s only site simply visit their website and provide them with your email and await your e-vite!

## 7. Bluefly.com

Bluefly.com carries thousands of high end brands at reduced prices. If you’re into more pricier brands, this is the website for you! From Giuseppe Zanotti, Tom Ford and Louboutin to Lanvin and Prada, Bluefly has it all. When you join their mailing list, you’ll receive $30 off of your first order of $150 or more. If that’s not a good enough reason to sign up and browse their site now, I don’t know what is!

Shopping on a budget is hard, but bargain shopping sites make it much more easier. Who thought it was this easy to be fashionable on a budget without having to sacrifice style. What are your favorite bargain shopping sites?
