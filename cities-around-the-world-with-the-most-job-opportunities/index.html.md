# 7 Cities around the World with the Most Job Opportunities ...

Do you want to know which are the cities with the most job opportunities? Well, thanks to PricewaterhouseCoopers’ (PwC) 2014 report on “Cities of Opportunity,” you can find quite a handy list of some of the cities around the world where you’ll have the most chances to find a job. In their analyses, they’ve used ten indicators to look at the factors that contribute to a well-balanced city, they’ve compared a number of 30 cities and they’ve ranked them based on job opportunities job seekers can find there. Here are 7 cities with the most job opportunities:

## 1. London

![London](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m0/h2/w6ca4yqt_600x375.jpg)

Among the cities with the most job opportunities, London ranks at number one, according to the people from PricewaterhouseCoopers. It is also home to the most billionaires worldwide and it has an economy focused on finance for international businesses. Some of the highest-paid jobs you can find there are senior executives, medical doctors and even marketing and sales directors.

## 2. Paris

![Paris](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/td/h1/w5ayduos_600x398.jpg)

This is one amazing city that can take your breath away. It’s full of respected universities, fantastic museums and exceptional libraries. It actually sits at the top of PricewaterhouseCoopers’ list for Intellectual Capital and Innovations. It has some of the lowest unemployment rates in Europe and individuals who work in human resources, sales or finance will find some of the highest salaries there.

## 3. New York

![New York](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qk/jq/vgj070v1_600x375.jpg)

New York is really thriving with job opportunities in a wide variety of industries; after all, it’s the largest city in the US. The study I mentioned in the beginning of this article ranked New York in the top three for the ease of doing business and second for most desired city for relocation.

## 4. Singapore

![Singapore](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/up/wu/otfxwbd4_473x338.jpg)

Another city with a lot of job opportunities for job seekers is Singapore which, according to the same study I mentioned earlier, ranks in the top three for transportation and infrastructure, city gateway and the ease of doing business. This is a fantastic place for a lot of people in a wide variety of industries and its economy has been ranked as the most open and least corrupt in the world.

## 5. Toronto

![Toronto](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gm/po/oi9131mr_600x356.jpg)

This is one of the cities in the world with the best reputation and also, with the most job opportunities. It has a high quality of life and it ranks in the top three for safety, health, infrastructure, security and transportation in the PricewaterhouseCoopers report. It’s also renowned for the ease of doing business and for its financial industry, with a big focus on banking and stocks.

## 6. Stockholm

![Stockholm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wj/ba/orhuwfsp_600x400.jpg)

This is one of the fastest growing cities in Europe with some of the best job opportunities in the world. It’s one of the best cities for start-ups and it’s actually booming with a lively tech industry, having almost 700 high-tech companies in the area. It also focuses a lot of its attention and resources on green energy and sustainability.

## 7. Hong Kong

![Hong Kong](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/se/ll/qelc7wvl_600x400.jpg)

This amazing city has one the highest life expectancies in the world, so you could clearly say that it’s one of the best cities in the world regarding the quality of life of its inhabitants. It’s ranked number one for economic freedom and number two for the ease of doing business in the
PricewaterhouseCoopers report.

There are a lot of amazing cities in the world that can offer you a ton of wonderful job opportunities. I just mentioned a few in this little article but I’m sure that there are more I could add to this list. Do you know any other cities around the world with great job opportunities?
