# 8 Excellent Ways You Can save on Power Bills ...

If you want to know how to save on power bills, you’ve come to the right place! This list of great tips and ways you can trim your power bill down to size will have you jumping for joy! You'll be amazed at all the little ways you can save on a power bill that you never knew existed! Power bills are like any other bill you owe, they must be paid. But this is one bill where you can partially control the amount you owe, so don’t be so glum! Read on for 8 genius ways you can save on a power bill.

## 1. Unplug Electronics

Leaving your electronics plugged up can slowly suck money out of your bank account via your power bill! Even thought the item isn’t in use, it still uses energy by being plugged up. You can save on a power bill by unplugging items like cell phone chargers, lamps, toasters, hair dryers, and other similar things when they are not in use.

## 2. Close Your Blinds

Sunlight streaming into a room can really heat it up, despite the AC’s best efforts to cool it down. Spare your unit from working overtime by pulling the blinds closed during the hottest part of the day. The same tip applies during the winter. Closing the blind can seal out little puffs of cold air that can push through the window frame and keep your heat inside the house!

## 3. Limit Appliance Use

This may sound like an odd tip to save on a power bill, but it really works! Using all your appliances at once, or during the hottest part of the day, will use more electricity and cause your AC to work overtime. It’s better to do your chores one at a time, and not during the afternoon. Choose early morning or late evening to run dishwashers or washing machines.

## 4. Buy Some Caulk

Here’s a great tip for saving on power bills! You may not be the handy type, but you can learn easily. You can even grab a neighbor or relative to do this for you. Get some caulk and use it to seal off all the cracks in your doors and windows. Cold winter air or summer heat seeping through the cracks will banish your money as quickly as you can turn around! Save those precious pennies and caulk those cracks!

## 5. Don’t Leave Lights on

Want to learn how to save on power bills? Start turning out lights in the rooms you aren’t using! You’d be surprised to know that by limiting your lights to the room you are in, you can save $50 or more per month on your power bill! A few other things to watch out for include leaving porch lights on all night, leaving lights on in the house while you are at work, or leaving lights on overnight.

## 6. Upgrade Your Appliances

How old are your appliances? If they are more than 10 years old, you are probably paying much more on your power bill than you should. Older versions of appliances tend to use more power, and therefore fatten your power bill up. It will be a little pricey initially, but if you upgrade to some newer appliances, you will end up saving money in the long run. You may even sell your old appliances for a small fortune!

## 7. Check Your Filters

One excellent way to save on a power bill is to check your filters monthly. If they are clogged, full, or worn out, you may not be getting maximum performance from your heating and air unit. It only takes a few minutes to change a filter, and the price for replacement filters are much slimmer than the price for a new unit. If you don’t take care of your unit, you will end up having to replace it!

## 8. Get Your Price

Before you give up and throw in the towel, figuring you will be paying an expensive power bill for the rest of your life, try this tip for saving on power bills. Give your utility company a call. Many companies will offer fixed rates if you have been with them for a while or you are always on time with your payments. At the very least, a fixed rate will prevent you from having to be so meticulous about watching your power consumption!

These tips for lowering your electricity usage and saving on power bills can save you up to $350 a year, if not more. It all depends on how frugal and strict you can be when applying these techniques. Do you have any tips or ways to save on a power bill? Please comment below; I’d love to hear your suggestions! I’m always on the lookout for a new method for how to save on power bills!
