# 8 Things to do if You Want to Start Your Own Business ...

Starting your own business is something many of us dream of. It sounds wonderful, doesn´t it – being your own boss? However, starting your own business is not that simple, and there are a lot of factors to bear in mind if your venture is to stand any chance of being a success. Here are some very important things to do if you want to start a business.

## 1. Research

One of the vital things to do if you want to start a business is to do a LOT of research. Look into whether there´s a market for your idea, whether it´s financially viable, and how much competition exists. Plus, it´s just as important to find out what not to do in order to avoid making mistakes. You can save yourself an awful lot of money and stress if you work out that the business you want to start isn´t likely to work.

## 2. Support

There is lots of help and advice available to people who want to start their own business. This includes courses and grants. Try small business organizations, college courses, the city council, and even the unemployment office. Take advantage of all the help you can find from people experienced in helping new small businesses.

## 3. Mentor

A mentor can be incredibly useful when you are starting your own business. Do you know someone who has already been down that route? Ask if they are willing to guide you. If you don´t know someone personally, then read books by business people who inspire you.

## 4. Funding

Most small businesses need at least some funding to get started, whether it´s for renting premises, building a website, buying stock or purchasing equipment. You will need to either fund it yourself from savings, obtain a loan from a bank or family, or apply for a grant (which will probably only cover part of the cost, assuming you get one).

## 5. Viability

It´s crucial to find out if your business is a viable idea. There´s no use starting yet another cupcake business if twenty people are already doing the same thing in the same town – unless you can offer something different (such as vegan cupcakes, or gluten-free). Similarly, luxury products will not do well if buyers don´t have much spare cash.

## 6. Ability

The would-be small businesswoman also needs to be honest about her own abilities. If your venture is to stand a chance of success, then it must be something that you can do and love. You can´t be a plumber if you don´t have training! Also, if you don´t like dealing with customers face to face, then a business like a shop or café will not suit you.

## 7. Training

If you would like to start a small business that involves a skilled trade, then look into getting the training. This is essential (and probably expensive). Without the appropriate training, you will not be able to obtain the necessary insurance. Besides, as any business relies on repeat trade, it´s vital to do an excellent job so that people will request your services again and recommend you to others.

## 8. Work Hard – and Even Harder!

Starting your own business is a great idea in principle – but you must be prepared to work much harder than you ever did in a regular job. It takes a long time to build up a business, and there are no days off. You will have to put in very long hours. Eventually this should pay off, but don´t even think about starting a business if you aren´t prepared to dedicate most of your time to it.

There are lots of things to do if you want to start a business. Being your own boss has lots of advantage, but it´s very important to be aware that it is not an easy option. Preparation, dedication and the ability to promote yourself are just some of the required attributes. Have you ever considered starting your own business, and what type of business would you like to run?
