# Why 🤔 We All Dream of Living a Life of Luxury ...

One of the most common dreams that people tell one another when they get asked about their goals and ambitions is to be rich enough to be able to live a life of total luxury. Sure, I get it, working hard and having to grind for success builds characters and stops us from being arrogant and complacent, but don’t lie to me: if you could live every day of your life like Beyoncé, you would be jumping for joy! There are so many factors that make for a dream existence. Here is **why we all dream of living a life of luxury**!

## 1. No Price Tag Check

We all dream of getting to that stage in life when you don’t actually have to check the price tag on anything, ever. Just imagine being able to walk through a store and add things to your basket without having to think about how those purchases will impact on your weekly budget!

## 2. Quality Interest Time

When you have more money than you know what to do with, you get to spend more time doing things that are solely related to your interests and hobbies. When people aren’t so focused on working and making money, they are able to expand their horizons in more pleasing extracurricular ways.

## 3. Needs Attended to

Gone are the days of having to do pretty much anything you don’t want to by yourself! When you have the funds, you can arrange for every aspect of your life to be catered to. You could even have your own driver if you can’t be bothered to do the hard travel work anymore!

## 4. Choosing Work Hours

If you already have enough money to be comfortable for life, then you get to be in the position of choosing when you work, and how long you work for. You no longer have to be a slave to the 9 to 5 machine.

## 5. Indulge in the Finer Things

You no longer have to think twice before treating yourself to something nice, or splashing some cash to have a great experience. That sort of indulging can become an every day thing for you!

## 6. Private Jet

One of the absolute best parts about living a life of luxury is the ability to hire a private jet whenever you need one. Once you have experienced that kind of hassle-free flying, you will never want to go back to boarding gates and queues again!

## 7. Best Locations

No matter where you feel like going in the world, your money means that you will always be able to choose the best location wherever you happen to be. No more searching for the best deals or making compromises!

## 8. Control

When you have the financial edge in life, you can be in total control of everything that happens around you. Your worries are gone.

## 9. It Can’t BuY Everything

HOWEVER, it is important to remember that money and luxury can’t buy you everything. Alongside your finances, you also need to work hard on your personal relationships to ensure that you are happy and have a close set of friends with whom you can share your lifestyle!
