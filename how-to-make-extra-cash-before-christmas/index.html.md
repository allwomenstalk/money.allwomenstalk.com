# How to Make Extra Cash before Christmas ...

This is the time of year when you need money the most. Otherwise, you won't be able to buy thoughtful gifts for your friends and family. That's why [Good Housekeeping](https://bit.ly/1ROrnZ6) has come up with a few ways to make some extra cash before Christmas:

## 1. Do Odd Jobs for Your Neighbors

![skin, chin, close up, hand, finger,](https://img.allw.mn/content/t7/of/r5ikkkjp5c10e52471dc3689314776_320x160.gif)

[Care](https://www.care.com/home-page-p1524.html?irclickid=xWKwRw0qcxyNRDR1flRGUUR9UkD3z6S8zX-90o0&irpid=27795&irgwc=1&_qs=1) and [Task Rabbit](https://www.taskrabbit.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) will help you find odd jobs. Meanwhile, [E Neighbor](https://bit.ly/1ROrljT) lets you make money by picking up a package for a neighbor. How simple is that?

## 2. Trade in Your Old Products

_ You have plenty of old books, CDs, and video games that you don't use anymore. Why not trade them into Amazon_ or [Game Stop](http://www.gamestop.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for a few bucks?

## 3. Have a Group Yard Sale

![Have a Group Yard Sale](https://img.allw.mn/content/ey/cq/hqlkdjcj56658ecc7733a183852780_500x280.gif)

You might not have enough stuff for a yard sale, which is why you should find some friends and have a yard sale together. The more items you're selling, the longer others will stop to browse.

## 4. Become a Secret Shopper

_ Best Mark_ and [Secret Shopper](https://www.secretshopper.com/) hire people to become mystery shoppers. All you have to do is walk into a store and see how you're treated.

## 5. Sell Your Stuff Online

![Sell Your Stuff Online](https://img.allw.mn/content/le/ze/t9bkqj0n56658ecbb8338608399084_500x281.gif)

Go to [Posh Mark](https://poshmark.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) or [Thread Flip](https://www.tripboba.com/) to sell your items. It's simple.

## 6. Walk Dogs

![shoulder, girl, hand, arm, muscle,](https://img.allw.mn/content/to/go/ji92lat85c10e52e8915e762641684_500x255.gif)

If you hate kids, become a pet sitter instead. You could even walk dogs for your neighbors. Try looking through [Rover](https://www.rover.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for options.

## 7. Sell Your Crafts

![Sell Your Crafts](https://img.allw.mn/content/pq/z4/f6dq0p5m56658ecbbec81550739094_450x338.gif)

Go to a local craft fair to sell your items. If that's not possible, sign up for [Etsy](https://www.etsy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) and sell your products there.

How are you planning on making more money before the holidays?
