## 1. Into the World of VMware and Its Certification Paths

To convince you on why associating your career in any of the certifications offered by VMware is valuable, let us first learn more about this renowned virtualization company and what it has to offer.

VMware has always been on the caliber plane of the virtualization market. It has laid open to the world of IT a certification program that sets itself apart from other organizations. ## 2. Everything You Need to Know about the Exam

2V0-622D is also known as VMware Certified Professional 6.5 - Data Center Virtualization Delta Exam. It consists of a total of 70 questions that are either in the single and multiple choice and proctored format. You are to finish all of the exam questions successfully within 105 minutes. If you get a score of 300, this means you’ve nailed the exam, consequently, the certification.

## 3. Skills Tested

The exam basically assesses whether you are equipped with the expertise in effectively managing VMware vSphere 6.5 environments. This includes your ability in dealing with vSphere Virtual Machines. Other than that, you must also show mastery in scaling, installing, and deploying a myriad of VMware vSphere 6.5 situations.

## 4. Associated Certification

• VMware Certified Professional 6.5 - Data Center Virtualization or VCP6.5-DCV

Successfully finishing the exam then leads you to this in-demand credential which validates your theoretical and real-world competence in efficiently overseeing a vSphere V6.5 infrastructure. As a holder of this certification, you must incorporate your implementing and troubleshooting skills in applying the innovative practices of VMware and virtualization.

• VMware Certified Professional - Data Center Virtualization 2019 or VCP-DCV 2019

Another certification related to this exam is the VCP-DCV 2019, which has gradually made waves in the virtualization market. Acquiring this credential demonstrates your know-how in filling the proper techniques, skills, and practices in a vSphere V6.7 infrastructure.

## 5. Prerequisites for the Exam

An applicant of this exam must first be well-versed in the vSphere environment since this certification is already on the professional level. Hence, you must have a solid foundation first in order to fully perform in vSphere 6.5 environments. This can be gained from a year-long experience in IT as well as months of working experience in vSphere using the necessary installation, configuration, and management skills.

## 6. Topics Covered in 2V0-622D

In comparison to Exam 2V0-622, the topics encompassed in 2V0-622D regulate more on the modifications between vSphere v6.0 and v6.5. Some of the objectives of the exam emphasize on the configuration of varied controls as well as securing and managing Virtual Machines. To get an in-depth understanding of the exam objectives, you can access the comprehensive study guide provided on the official website of VMware.
