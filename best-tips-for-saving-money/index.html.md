# 9 of the Best Tips for Saving Money for Girls Needing a Rainy Day Fund ...

What are the **best tips for saving money**?

Money is one of the most important assets in our life. Without this, we cannot imagine our life. It is required at every step, starting from the first day when we are born till the last day when we stop breathing. But, almost all of us have faced a money crisis at least once. This can be avoided if you start saving a little each day. You can save and still have the cash for the things you want and need.

If you are just starting to save, then you should definitely know the **best tips for saving money**. This can make a huge change in your life. So what are you waiting for?

## 1. KEEP a RECORD of YOUR EXPENSES

When you are spending, it is very important that you keep a record of your expenses. Keep track of every penny that you spend, including the newspaper, the coffee you drink, money spent on the mortgage, gas and anything else. That way you can easily see where you can make cuts to start putting some cash away. This is one of the **best tips for saving money!**

## 2. MAKe a STRICT MONEY SAVING BUDGET

When you have started to earn money, the most important thing that you should do is to start making a budget. This will help you to have an idea on how much you will save and how much you will spend. Most of the newbie who has just started to earn money, budgeting can be very important. Budget making is very important. This is how you can keep track of your income.

## 3. MAKE a PLAN on SAVING the MONEY

If you make money, you should make a budget that includes a savings category. Keep 10-15% of your income in your savings. If you can't do that, have a look at your expenses and see where you are spending too much. That money can then be moved into your savings account.

## 4. MAKE a LIST of YOUR PRIORITIES

Be sure that you are making long-term goals, like your retirement plan and life insurance. Giving priority to your goals is an important part in your life. Perhaps you need a new car or want to buy a house. Having your goals written down can be more motivating when it comes to spending or saving.

## 5. WATCH YOUR SAVINGS GROW

Keeping an eye on how your nest egg is growing can help motivate you to keep going. It can also help you see where you can increase the amount and when you reach your goals.

## 6. CONSIDER the LATTE FACTOR to save MONEY

This is one of the most important steps in your saving plan. If you are someone who spends money on daily vices like a latte or smoothie then you should fix an amount of the money that you will spend on these items. Once it's gone you have to make your own.

## 7. TRANSFER MONEY to YOUR SAVINGS ACCOUNT

Set up an automatic deposit into your savings account. When you get paid, the money can transfer right in so you won't be tempted to spend it. This is super easy and will help you grow your savings account in no time at all.

## 8. CHOOSE the RIGHT TYPEs of TOOLS

One of the biggest ways to save money is to make use of the right tools. Choose a high yield savings account that will give you a high interest rate. Work with your bank to find the tools that will help you save the most money.

## 9. UNSUBSCRIBE to the THINGS THAT TEMPT YOU

Get rid of subscriptions to magazines, cable television and other things that you don't really use. You'll save a bunch of money and you can check out television programs and magazines at the library for free.

Reducing your expenses slowly can make a huge change in your life. Saving money can help you reduce the chances of losing control and having a financial crisis. Start saving today.
