# How to Get the Most out of Your Paypal Account ...

PayPal is becoming more and more popular, because plenty of people get paid over the computer. However, even though it's popular, that doesn't mean that it's completely safe. You still need to be careful, so that you don't end up losing out on your money. Here are a few safety tips that'll help you get the most out of your PayPal account:

## 1. Don’t Click on Links from Emails That PayPal Sends You

![Don’t Click on Links from Emails That PayPal Sends You](https://img.allw.mn/content/ud/r9/awdlel6l56c720b140ff9689241545_500x370.gif)

If you get an email from PayPal, don't open it up and click on the links inside. There are scammers who will send out fake emails in order to get access to your private information. If there's anything that PayPal needs to alert you of, you can find out about it on their actual website. That means there's no reason for you to click on sketchy links.

## 2. Don’t Attach Your PayPal Account to Your Bank Account

![Don’t Attach Your PayPal Account to Your Bank Account](https://img.allw.mn/content/kq/ms/zxl2lpkr56c720b174742726348341_444x250.gif)

Some people will advise you to avoid attaching your PayPal account to your bank account, because if someone manages to hack your PayPal, they could possibly empty your bank account as well.

## 3. Keep an Eye on Your Balance

![Keep an Eye on Your Balance](https://img.allw.mn/content/lu/ur/s45x7kl556c720b1316bc690937311_500x267.gif)

Don't blindly assume that your PayPal account has the accurate amount of money in it. Check it as often as possible to make sure that you're not getting ripped off. If someone is taking money out of your account, you'll be able to see the withdrawal on your main page. If you're not getting paid the right amount by your boss or buyers, you'll be able to see that, too.

## 4. Create a Secure Password

![Create a Secure Password](https://img.allw.mn/content/tg/nq/cfmy6sa256c720b2583ac275022848_500x250.gif)

You don't want to use the same password for your PayPal as you do for every other website you visit. Make sure that your money is safe by coming up with something that is secure. That means you should use as many letters, numbers, and symbols as you can. You're better safe than sorry.

## 5. Only Buy Products from Websites You Can Trust

![Only Buy Products from Websites You Can Trust](https://img.allw.mn/content/zu/co/nkhw1xb556c720b1aea3e515458076_460x259.gif)

If you're going to use your PayPal account to pay for something, make sure the website you're buying items from is legitimate. You don't want to end up getting screwed over. Do your research on the website you're browsing before you purchase anything from it.

## 6. Don’t Log onto Your Account with a Public Computer

![Don’t Log onto Your Account with a Public Computer](https://img.allw.mn/content/bf/xr/o4nlgvxg56c720b1606ee516004777_400x300.gif)

If you're going to be managing your money, you should do so on your own, private computer. You don't want to log into your account on a public computer at your local library, because theres's always a chance that someone else will be able to access it after you. Do the safe thing by sticking to using your own electronics, and make sure that you use your own private wifi as well.

## 7. Use a Unique Username

![Use a Unique Username](https://img.allw.mn/content/rw/ya/uk08opjv56c720b2829d5850413139_500x281.gif)

You already know that your password for PayPal should be unique. However, your username should be unique, as well. That way, you'll make it even more difficult for others to find their way into your account and abuse your money. So come up with something clever!

PayPal is a convenient way to manage your money. However, you don't want to end up losing all of that money, which is why you should follow these simple tips for keeping your money safe. What other advice do you have for PayPal users?
