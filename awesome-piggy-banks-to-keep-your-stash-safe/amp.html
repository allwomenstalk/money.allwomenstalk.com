7 Awesome Piggy Banks to Keep Your Stash Safe ...

Break in Case of Emergency • Basic Counter • Car Guy • Favorite Character • Automatic Wrapper • More ...

By Holly

If you want to save money in style, you'll need some awesome piggy banks. Some of them are more traditional, while others use technology to add a unique twist. Either way, they're all great ways to save up your extra change. Check out these awesome piggy banks and be prepared to buy one.

1. Break in Case of Emergency

If you've ever wanted to pretend you were in a movie, you'll love this breakable bank. You can store your coins in it until you need to get them out. When you do, your only choice is to smash through to get your stash. Buy one of these awesome piggy banks if you want something fun and temporary. If you check out Amazon, you can buy one for only $15.

2. Basic Counter

This is a true piggy bank. It's in the shape of your favorite farm animal and makes for an adorable accessory for your room. It even counts your money and tells you how much you have. You no longer have to look at your coin jar and guess how much it adds up to. With this bank, all the work is done for you. All you have to do is remember to keep feeding it yummy coins.

3. Car Guy

This is for all of you car lovers. The more coins that are in this bank, the higher the fuel gauge moves. This is a perfect way to save up some gas money. It's also a cute gift idea for the men in your life. Buy one at makemorecents.com if you think that this is the bank for you.

4. Favorite Character

Coin banks come in all different shapes and sizes. If you're a fangirl, buy a bank that looks like your favorite character. There are plenty of different designs out there, from The Avengers characters to Disney inspired banks. Search Amazon for whatever best suits your interests. If you like what the bank looks like, there's a greater chance you'll put it somewhere noticeable and remember to fill it every day.

5. Automatic Wrapper

If you want your money sorted, this is the bank for you. It’s electronic and will automatically put your coins into paper wrappers. It’s easy to use and tells you how much money you’ve accumulated. So why not buy one and let it do all of the work for you? If you're interested, they’re available at Bed Bath & Beyond.

6. Couples Coin Collector

Do you need something adorable to sit on your nightstand? If you're in a relationship, this is a cute and funny idea. Whatever money goes into your man's bank will automatically fall into yours. Of course, you shouldn't actually hog all of his money. You know the phrase: sharing is caring.

7. Oversized

If you're planning on saving up for a trip, a little bank just won't do. You'll need something bigger and capable of carrying enough coins for a plane ticket. There are plenty of oversized banks at banksbanksbanks.com. If you get one of these babies, make sure that you really love the design. Since it's so large, it'll be hard to miss.

Buying a piggy bank can help save you cash. Otherwise, you might end up losing perfectly good change that adds up over time. Which one of these banks is your favorite?
