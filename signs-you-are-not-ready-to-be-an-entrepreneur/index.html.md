# 7 Signs You Are Not Ready to Be an Entrepreneur ...

If you think about quitting your day job and you plan on starting and running your own business, you should still know that there are a few signs you are not ready to be an entrepreneur just yet. Just like Conner Forrest said, “The life as a startup founder is a rocky one.” You need to spend a lot of time working on your business and sometimes you have to put in a lot of energy just to obtain a couple of insignificant results. Entrepreneurship can be a journey that some people are not ready to embark on and sometimes it’s best to just put your startup dreams on hold until you have all you need to turn your business into a success. Here are a few obvious signs you are not ready to be an entrepreneur:

## 1. You Don’t Have a Support System

One of the most obvious signs you are not ready to be an entrepreneur is the fact that you don’t have a support system, made of friends, family, spouse or colleagues who are ready to support you in taking this leap. If you want to be successful, you need to have an established network of people who are willing to support you, so you won’t make poor decisions based only on personal reasons.

## 2. You Have a Paycheck Dependency

When you start up a business, you should know that you won’t make too much money right away, so before you decide to quit your job and become an entrepreneur, just see if you can go without a salary for six months. If you don’t have any money saved, then it’s best to wait a little longer before you decide to take this big step in your career.

## 3. You Have Professional ADD

If you can be best described as the type of person who has professional ADD, which means that you are easily bored and you get easily frustrated when you can’t reach your goals, then maybe entrepreneurship is not for you. As an entrepreneur, you have to do a lot of things that are not very interesting and that won’t give you too much satisfaction, but you just have to do them quite often if you want your business to be successful.

## 4. You Get Stage Fright

If you want to start your own business, you should be aware of the fact that you are the primary spokesperson for your company. If you have stage fright or social anxiety, first you should try to work on those issues and then start your own company, since you should learn how to feel comfortable in the spotlight and how to like public speaking if you want your business to be a hit.

## 5. You Don’t Believe in Marketing

If you don’t believe in marketing, then you are not ready yet to become an entrepreneur. Marketing is in fact what makes the business world go round, so if you don’t embrace it, you might find it hard to succeed. You have to spend a lot of money and you have to dedicate a lot of effort to build your own business, and marketing is one of those expenses that you have to make.

## 6. You Think Complexity is “Cool”

If you think that complexity is the answer to everything, then you might be wrong since sometimes, the simplest solutions are the most efficient and the most successful. Just like Nicole Fallon said, “As a business owner, you need the ability to distill concepts to their simplest forms so they can be easily communicated.”

## 7. You Are a Problem Passer

If you are usually a problem passer then it’s best to not become an entrepreneur just yet. As a business owner, you’ll have to make a lot of decisions. Some people, like your customers or your employees, might not agree with them, but you should still do what’s best for your business.

Being an entrepreneur is not that easy and there are quite a few qualities you have to possess in order to be successful. Do you know any other signs that someone is not ready to be an entrepreneur? Do tell!
