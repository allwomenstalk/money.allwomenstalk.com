# The Best Deals You Can Find Are at Costco ...

Want to know **the best deals at Costco**? Have you stepped inside a bulk store lately, like Costco, Sam’s Club or BJ’s? There are deals to be had inside, but, if you’re not careful, you’ll go over your set shopping budget. I’m taking you inside my local Costco to show you the best of the best deals on everything from granola bars to toilet paper. If it isn’t a good deal, it won’t make my list.

Believe it or not, there is a “way” to shop in these stores. Take these tips to heart when you’re pushing that giant cart from one end of the store to the other, and you’ll be a “Costco Pro” just like me! Here are **the best deals at Costco**.

## 1. Two Thumbs up on the Clothes

One of the **the best deals at Costco** is the clothing. The quality of clothes at Costco is great. I don’t buy tons of clothes there, but the items I have purchased have held up just as well, if not better, than my higher priced clothing.

## 2. Returns Are Really Amazing

This isn’t officially on the books at Costco, but I can tell you from personal experience that they are wonderful when it comes to returns. Just make sure you have the packaging of whatever it is you want to return along with your ID and membership card and 99% of the time, you can make a return or exchange with no problems….even without a receipt.

## 3. Go in with a Game Plan

Have a list and do your best to stick to it. There are so many cool gadgets and a plethora of great sales to be had, so if you’re not mindful of your spending, you can easily double or triple your planned bottom line. I keep a running Costco list on my phone and simply highlight what I plan to buy before I get there. This way, I don’t have to make a new list every time I go…. which, is probably more often than I care to admit!

## 4. Don’t Forget about the Freezer

What good do 4 loaves of bread do if they're just going to get moldy? Same goes for cheese, veggies, meats, and so many other things you can buy in bulk. Have a plan to portion out your bulk purchases into freezer bags as soon as you get home and keep handy only what you can use before it goes bad.
