# 7 Jobs That Require Little or No Experience ...

If you are fresh out of school and you are looking for a job but you are lacking experience, here are a few jobs that require no experience. Even though a lot of companies nowadays require a college diploma and years of experience in that field, you can still find high paying jobs that don’t require too much experience. Some of these jobs will even offer you paid, on-the-job training, so what more could you want? I should mention the fact that all those jobs that require little experience or post-secondary education are not all in retail stores and restaurants. Here are 7 jobs that require no experience that you should consider:

## 1. Bill Collector

One of those jobs that require no experience that you should consider is bill collector, since most of these positions only require a high school diploma. Also, experts estimate that the employment rate for bill collectors will grow by approximately 19% in the next 10 years. In order to get this job, you must demonstrate good customers service skills and you must effectively communicate over the phone.

## 2. Bartender

You don’t need a college education to become a bartender but you must go to a bartending school if you want to excel at your job. Bartending can be hard though, because this job requires spending long hours at work and you must deal with an imbibing public from different walks of life. If you want to get more details, you should know that the ABC Bartending School lists bartending school locations around the US.

## 3. School Bus Driver

If you have enough patience and if you love kids, you could become a school bus driver since this job requires no previous experience. It also comes with certain benefits (like the fact that you get the same holidays and days off that students enjoy) and a decent salary. Of course, in order to get this job, you must have clean driving and criminal records.

## 4. Real Estate Broker

Another job you can get without having too much experience is real estate broker. You should know that it does take a few years to make money in this field but there is great potential and you only need an agent license to get accepted into a real estate firm. To become eligible for that agent license, you must take a 60-hour course and once you are employed, you must take another test to become a full-fledged broker.

## 5. Human Resources Assistant

A very decent job that requires little experience is human resources assistant. This position will require you to keep track of all the information regarding a company’s employees and you must also help the human resources manager in other activities that involve company and employee relations. Most times, a high school diploma is the only requirement for this job and it will also offer you a lot of opportunities for advancement in your career.

## 6. Library Technician

If you love books and reading and if you are looking for a job that requires little or no experience, you could become a library technician. Even though becoming a librarian requires years of schooling and certifications, in order to become a library technician, in most cases you must have a high school diploma. Technicians assist librarians in ordering new titles, shelving books and organizing materials.

## 7. Occupational Therapist Aide

This is another job you could obtain with little or no experience. An occupational therapist aide must prepare the materials and the equipment used in the rehabilitation of people with mental, physical, emotional or even developmental disabilities. It just requires a high school diploma and good interpersonal skills.

There are a lot of jobs that you can get that require little or no experience. I just mentioned a few in this little article but I’m sure that there are many more I could add to this list. Do you know any other decent jobs that require no experience? Please tell us about them in the comments section!
