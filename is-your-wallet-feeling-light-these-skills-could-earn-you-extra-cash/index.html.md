# Is Your Wallet Feeling Light? These Skills Could Earn You Extra Cash ...

There are many skills and talents that could earn you extra cash and if your wallet is feeling particularly light at the moment, you might just have one or two of these skills which could make your wallet look a little healthier in the coming months.

## 1. Lovely Languages

Having a knowledge of other languages is something that might be able to earn you some extra cash. Is there anyone in your college, university, work place or near your home who is looking for a language tutor? Make enquiries and see if anyone is looking to learn a new language as it's one of the great ways to earn a little extra cash.

## 2. Marvellous Musical Instruments

If you play a musical instrument then this is a great skill to have and many people know the benefits of learning to play a musical instrument. Whether it's the piano, guitar, flute or any of the many other musical instruments out there, you can use your talents and skills to earn a little extra money.

## 3. Sewing

Do you sew? Could you sell your makes? Maybe you could do alterations for family and friends. Sewing is a very therapeutic pass-time and you could use your creativity to make some money.

## 4. Cheeky Child-Minding

Child-minding takes patience and this is something not everyone has. If you're skilled when it comes to looking after the little ones maybe you can use your child-whispering skills to make a little extra cash. Of course, many parents and guardians look for qualifications before they will put their trust in another person but everyone needs a baby-sitter at some point.

## 5. Gorgeous Gardening

Do you enjoy gardening? Not everyone has an eye for detail when it comes to gardening so don't dismiss this skill if you love all things floral. Maybe you can make a little extra cash by doing some gardening and landscaping for people in your area or simply weed and cut the grass for an elderly neighbour.

## 6. Fabulously Floral

Flower arranging is another fabulous skill which not everyone can do. Do you have an eye for colour when it comes to flowers? Many people need flowers for weddings, birthdays and other occasions.

## 7. Crazy Cake Decorating

Cake decorating is super fun and if you have watched those great shows about the master cake decorators and their fabulous feats, you will know that it truly is an art form. Perhaps you're gifted with the piping bags and are able to see the many sculptural opportunities when it comes to decorating cakes. Family and friends always need cakes for birthdays, weddings and other special occasions.

## 8. Wondrous Writing

Perhaps you have a passion for writing and would like to use your skills as a wordsmith to earn a little extra cash. Writing is a difficult profession to make a living from and is certainly something that should be done for the love of it rather than for the sole-purpose of making money. Blogging is something that you could also consider but again, with the millions of talented writers on the Blogosphere, you need to not only have a keen eye for detail but the ability to think of something which isn't being blogged about by thousands of others.

## 9. Awesome Animal Sitting

If you love animals and see yourself as something of an animal whisperer, perhaps you can use your skills to walk dogs in the neighbourhood or feed animals when their owners are at work or on holiday. Being around animals is great fun and can make you feel so relaxed so what better way to earn a little extra cash?

These are just a few of the skills and talents that could earn you some extra cash. Can you think of any more?
