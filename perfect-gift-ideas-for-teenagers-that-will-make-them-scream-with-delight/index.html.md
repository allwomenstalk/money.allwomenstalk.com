# 7 Perfect Christmas Gift Ideas for Teenagers That Will Make Them Scream with Delight ...

It can be really hard to find **Christmas gifts for teenagers**. You always want to get a gift that will put a real smile on someone’s face, and not that forced smile that they give so you won’t feel bad! So if you are having trouble picking out a present, here are some of the best **Christmas gifts for teenagers** that will hopefully have them screaming with delight.

## 1. Polaroid Camera

![girl, long hair, black hair, electronic device, selfie,](https://img.allw.mn/content/vm/p0/n2l4ffuc5a31713171224343497736_500x200.gif)

Polaroid cameras are officially back! Recently they've become a buzz among the younger generation because, well, they are cool. The pictures come out instantly and have a beautiful effect that we try to get using camera effect apps. It’s unconventional and unique, and that’s exactly what teens want. Polaroid cameras have gotten an upgrade since the days of old, making them one of the absolute best **Christmas gifts for teenagers**.

## 2. Concert Tickets

![crowd, people, stage, audience, concert,](https://img.allw.mn/content/lm/a2/iwisb95a5a31714badbdf765906921_500x281.gif)

Adolescents are obsessed with music as it serves as a sort of an escape. Songs describe your feelings perfectly and put your thoughts into words. So no matter what genre or type of music your adolescent enjoys, he or she would definitely enjoy concert tickets for his or her favorite artist or band. A couple of tickets also gives you chance to spend some time together or seen your teen off with a bestie.

## 3. Books

![library, library science, bookcase, public library, shelving,](https://img.allw.mn/content/t0/z3/v9jg8t5h5a31716ad6768118544721_500x282.gif)

Giving books as presents is classic. Often times teenagers love to read but refuse to spend saved-up money on a book, so you can help them out this Christmas. Pick books that you think he or she should read or just look up reviews for teen novels online. A bookmark is also a great way to please the bookworm in your life.

## 4. Clothes

![clothing, shoulder, girl, long hair, thigh,](https://img.allw.mn/content/yj/ua/hse9vxhe5c065265dd274119454161_400x225.gif)

You can never go wrong with buying clothes as gifts because teens love clothes. They are in the middle of finding their personal style, which makes it really fun to choose clothes for them. If you are aware of an item that your teen has been eyeing for weeks, go for it. However, if you don’t want to risk buying a bad gift, just get them a gift card to their favorite clothing store.

## 5. Jewelry

![human hair color, beauty, jewellery, lip, neck,](https://img.allw.mn/content/gh/gn/mibmjx8p5a31726e40663796312014_500x241.gif)

This gift idea may apply more toward girls because you know what they say, ‘diamonds are a girl’s best friend’. However, there is no need to spend a fortune on diamonds. It’s absolutely possible to get a quality jewelry piece at an affordable price. And jewelry for teen boys isn't all that hard to find these days either. Something handmade is always a thoughtful gift idea.

## 6. Tech Accessories

![hair, human hair color, beauty, hairstyle, hair coloring,](https://img.allw.mn/content/cu/b2/lvicfnpl5a3179c5a0e6e263781524_480x270.gif)

If you've been noticing that your teen has been complaining about a cracked iPod or a broken cell phone case, this is your opportunity to get a useful gift. Something to make their tech gadgets more fun and practical will always create a smile on Christmas morning.

## 7. Anything Personalized

![pink, red, purple, magenta, violet,](https://img.allw.mn/content/np/td/h46hb9mn5c0656eb8ecf6229986454_800x400.gif)

A personalized gift comes from the heart and is a good way to choose something that your teen will love, but that has an age-appropriate personal touch as well. There are loads of things you can personalize, so the sky is the limit.

Shopping for teenagers can be hard, especially if you are not a teenager yourself, so hopefully, this post gave you some ideas. What would make a great present in your opinion?
