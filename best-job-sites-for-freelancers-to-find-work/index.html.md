# Top 20 Best Job Sites for Freelancers to Find Work ...

There are so many **job sites for freelancers**, but how do you know which one are good or not? I have been a freelancer for 6 years and as a freelancer, I want to share some of the legit sites that you can use to earn money. Being a freelancer isn't always easy, but using the right resources can help. Here are the best **job sites for freelancers.**

Upwork is one of the big freelancing sites for hiring freelancers.
Site Link: _upwork.com_

Site Link: [99designs.com](https://99designs.com/)
