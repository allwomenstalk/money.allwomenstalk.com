# Awesome Ways to Still Be Happy when You're Broke ...

Are you money-free this weekend and you don’t know what to do? It can be really hard to figure out **how to still be happy when you're broke.** Well, don’t stress out because there is always a solution. There are lots of free activities that let you have fun without spending anything. Learning **how to still be happy when you're broke** won't be easy, but you'll love the freedom of creating a life you love, even if you don't have a million dollars in the bank.

## 1. See What You Have

![sea, shore, beach, vacation, ocean,](https://img.allw.mn/content/kp/er/d9k4mvfi59c18a3787537947659082_400x225.gif)

One of the best ways to cope when you're wondering **how to still be happy when you're broke** is to consider what you have. Are you homeless person with no electricity and living on the street? Do you have 3 meals every day? Do you have enough clothes to wear? If the answer to this is yes, then probably you are not broke and you will realize that you can have fun even when your budget is empty. You can sit on the couch, eat popcorn and watch a good movie. At least you have money for popcorn, right?

## 2. You Can Always do Cheap and Fun Stuff

![vertebrate, tree, emotion, fun, plant,](https://img.allw.mn/content/hi/vf/xujm2nvh59c18a42e1a64354672776_355x200.gif)

Certainly there are a lot of fun things you can do without needing money. You can camp in the backyard of your home, read a book from the library or go for a bike ride.

## 3. Do Not Worry Too Much about the Bills

![street, road, girl, tourism, recreation,](https://img.allw.mn/content/vv/xp/vj5lft5c59c18a0ee1c26605672893_474x266.gif)

Trying to be cheerful through hard times is what actually guides you in life. Do not stress too much if you can’t pay the phone or electricity bill. You can’t let that take over your life.

## 4. Laugh More

![blue, technology, electric blue, computer wallpaper, product,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ud/qb/ksif8b9959c18a7ab225d565142369_384x207.gif)

Laugh more and worry less. It will be the best medicine for your soul. Appearances and money don't define you. You can be happier than some billionaire and you need to learn how to do that.
