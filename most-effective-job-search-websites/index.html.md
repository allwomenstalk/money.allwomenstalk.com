# 7 Most Effective Job Search Websites ...

If you are in the market for a new job, then you should check out this list of the most effective job search websites since all these job search tools have some unique features which will maximize your chances of getting a job. These websites do more than just provide a database of job listings to search; they host your resume and they make it visible to employers and they will help you find the most current job openings fast. These search tools are simple and easy to use and they will help you land that new dream job as soon as possible. Here are some of the most effective job search websites:

## 1. Monster.com

Monster.com is one of the oldest job search websites out there. Even if some of its usefulness has diminished over the years (too many posts by spammy recruiters) it’s still a very important site on which to conduct a job search. This website serves just about every country around the world and it allows you to narrow your search by keywords, location and employer.

## 2. Indeed.com

This is a very solid web search engine. It’s an amazing website that is totally free for job seekers and it has listings aggregated from thousands of company websites and job boards across all fields. Their job search features are very easy to use and you can subscribe to job alerts via e-mail.

## 3. CareerBuilder.com

This wonderful website is among the leading job boards and it provides resume posting, job listing, and career advice and resources to job seekers. So basically it gives you the opportunity to find a job, to post a resume (you can store up to five resumes and cover letters), you get job advice and you can even look up job fairs.

## 4. LinkedIn.com

Even though you may not think to look for a job on this website, try to keep in mind that this is a professional social network so your profile is essentially your resume. You should know that employers can list jobs on this website to recruit the best candidates, those listings are available for you to search and some companies will even let you apply with nothing more than your LinkedIn profile.

## 5. SimplyHired.com

This is one of the world’s largest search engines that connects job seekers and employers through its powerful search engine platform. It pulls listings from thousands of websites across the world including job boards, newspapers, company career sites, non-profit organizations and government sites. Creating an account on this website will allow you to name, save and manage searches and save notes about the jobs you’ve viewed and that interest you.

## 6. Dice.com

This is a job search engine mostly dedicated to technology jobs. It’s basically the leading career website for technology and engineering professionals and it has thousands of listings at every IT level, from entry to senior. This website is also great for getting career advice and for analyzing technology trends.

## 7. Glassdoor.com

This is a free career community that helps individuals find jobs and companies recruit top talent. It gives you an inside look at jobs and employers and the content includes salary reports, interview questions and company assessments. You can also see the latest job listings and you can even get access to CEO approval ratings and to office photos.

There are a lot of web search engines out there that could help you land that dream job. I just mentioned a few here but I’m sure that there are many more I could add to this list. Do you know any other effective job search websites? Please tell us about them in the comments section!
