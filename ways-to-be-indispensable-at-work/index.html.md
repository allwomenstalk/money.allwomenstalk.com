# 7 Ways to Be Indispensable at Work ...

In today’s economy, no one’s job is secure, so it’s best to know all kinds of ways to be indispensable at work. A lot of companies decide to outsource their positions to cut costs, so job security is a serious concern for many employees all around the world. Being indispensable at work means that the productivity of your department might suffer without you and that’s why you should show your supervisors or your co-workers that you are essential to the overall success of the company, that you are so good and efficient at your job that they can’t even imagine replacing you. This will put you in a comfort zone because you won’t have to worry as much about losing your job. Here are 7 ways to be indispensable at work that you should consider:

## 1. Go the Extra Mile

One of the most efficient ways to be indispensable at work is by being willing and capable to go that extra mile. Show your boss that you are a valuable asset to the company and always try to do a little more than what is expected of you. You should try to go above and beyond your job description and be willing to work on things no other employee wants to work on.

## 2. Save Your Company Money

You should try to find new ways to save your company money. Do some research and look for ways you can help your company reduce some of their costs. For example, if they want to upgrade their hardware of software systems, try to make some recommendations and tell them about cheaper alternatives.

## 3. Be More Productive

Try to boost your productivity and always try to accomplish more of your tasks during the day. Be thorough, professional and fair with your work since being more productive when it comes to doing your job does not mean you look for short cuts.

## 4. Build Relationships

Make more connections, and try to build and nurture relationships with people who matter, people who are key decision makers because this can take you a long way. If you want to build strong work relationships, improve your listening skills, be a good communicator, be trustworthy and more empathetic towards your colleagues.

## 5. Treat Everyone with Respect

Genuine respect can take you a long way in your career because success is not only about how well you do your job but also, about the manner in which you treat your peers, your team members and your clients. Treat everyone as you would like to be treated and be nice with everyone around you.

## 6. Do Work That Matters

Don’t run away from difficult tasks! Always try to do the work that matters and not the one that’s easy. A lot of employees find tasks at work to stay busy, to pass the time so they could fly under their boss’s radar. If you want to be indispensable at work, tackle first the projects that matter most and that could assure your success.

## 7. Monopolize a Particular Skill

When you’re at work, try to find some task that needs to be done and make sure that you are the only one who knows how to do it. This could happen when a new initiative is created that requires a certain skill the organization has never had before or when somebody in your company who has a unique skill leaves and you step in their place.

If you are ambitious and if you take pride in your work, you should strive to be indispensable because you will be able to enjoy your job more knowing how valuable you are. Do you know any other ways to be indispensable at work? Please tell us about them in the comments section!
