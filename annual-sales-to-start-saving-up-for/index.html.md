# 7 Annual Sales to Start Saving up for  ...

Ladies, we all know we have a little bit of a problem when it comes to shopping. At times we can be impulsive and very irrational with money but it’s time we playing smart and instead of buying a few things here and there every other day, let’s save up for the biggest annual sales of the year and justify going all out!

## 1. Victoria’s Secret Semi Annual Sale

Nothing is more important than cute and comfortable undergarments but buying them at regular prices is a little bit ridiculous for most of us. However, there’s no reason to stray away from Victoria’s Secret! The key is waiting it out until their semi-annual sale, where you can get the same stuff for a significantly cheaper price. The sale actually happens two times a year, in June and January so look out!

## 2. Zara Semi-Annual Sale

Zara is a big time favorite store to shop at for a large demographic of people. They have everything from casual to business casual to cocktail, all with a very trendy spin on it, which is why it’s so high in demand despite the pricing. The sale happens near the end of June and at the end of December, and usually lasts for over several weeks!

## 3. Nordstrom Anniversary Sale

And if you have your eyes set on brand names, Nordstrom holds an Anniversary Sale in July! The prices drop down significantly low, finally making some of your dreams items very affordable. Just make sure to mark down the specific date and keep in mind that items sell out fast.

## 4. Saks Fifth Avenue Semi-Annual Sale

Saks Fifth Avenue outlets are a goldmine but unfortunately they’re not within the reach for a lot of people. For this reason, their semi-annual sale should not be missed! You can find high-end items sold a discounted price for up to 70 percent off at the nearest Saks near you. The sale runs through the end of June, so don’t miss out!

## 5. Forever 21 Semi-Annual Sale

Forever 21 is a great place to buy trendy items without making too much damage to your bank account. However, with the semi-annual sale on top, items are sold for as much as 50% off, making your favorite items super cheap! In case you are interested, the sale usually happens in June.

## 6. Bath & Body Works Semi-Annual Sale

Lotions, hand sanitizers, bronzers, sprays, you name it! Everything is on sale and up for grabs during the Bath & Body Works Semi-Annual Sale. You’ll be surprised just how many people take advantage of this event and stock up for the year. Don’t be the only one to miss out and get your savings ready for January and June.

## 7. The Children’s Place Monster Sale

And for all of you mommies out there, we know just how fast your kids grow out of their clothes. Therefore, one major sale to look out for is at The Children’s Place during the months of January and June or July. You’ll be able to find a ton of cute stuff at great prices, so keep your eyes peeled!

Which of these sales are you the most excited for?
