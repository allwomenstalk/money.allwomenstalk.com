# 7 Ways Adults Can Also Have Fun Back to School Shopping ...

Do you remember how much fun it was to go back to school shopping? You got to have a new wardrobe and new school supplies, which sometimes included colored pencils and crayons. Maybe you even got to have a snazzy new lunch box with fun cartoon characters. While you may not want a lunch box with cartoon characters on it, you can still have fun with back to school shopping as an adult.

## 1. New Clothes

Does it really matter if you haven’t grown two inches taller and don’t fit into your old clothes anymore? As an adult, you still need new clothes for the changing season, and getting new clothes is a great way to go back to school shopping as an adult. You go to your favorite clothing store and get a new outfit or a few great pieces that you have been dying to have.

## 2. New Shoes

Not that you really need an excuse to get another fabulous pair of shoes, but embracing the back to school shopping fever does provide one. Kids everywhere are getting new shoes for when they go back to school, and you can get in on the fun too. Get yourself a great pair of shoes that expresses your style, but you can still wear at work. This way, you feel both practical and stylish.

## 3. Replace Your Old Computer

In September, computer stores always have great back to school sales. It is the perfect time to replace an old computer, especially if your computer is ten years old. Before you go to the store, be sure to do your research so you get a computer that fits all of your needs. Then, you will be sure to make the most of the back to school deals.

## 4. Fun Office Supplies

When you were in school, there was always a required list of school supplies that you needed. Now that you are out of school, you can get office supplies instead. However, there is no need to get boring sticky notes and paper clips. Have fun and get colored paper clips or a stapler that is in the shape of a character. It will make working just a little more fun.

## 5. Get a Great Fall Coat

By fall most kids have outgrown their coat from last year, and they need a new one. You may not have outgrown your coat, but you still get a stylish new coat for fall. This year, try something different and get a jewel tone coat or an oversized coat that you can belt.

## 6. Toss the Brown Paper Lunch Bags

If you take your lunch to work, you should stop using boring brown paper lunch bags. Just because you are an adult, it doesn’t mean you can’t have a little more fun with your lunch. You can pick out whatever style of lunch box you want. Perhaps you would like to get a bento style lunch box?

## 7. Closet Organizers

Instead of getting items to organize and decorate a locker, you can embrace back to school shopping by getting some items to organize your closet. Shoe racks and sweater hangers can make a world of difference in a packed closet. Flocked velvet hangers that save space are also wonderful. They help your clothes actually fit in your closet! You don’t need much to organize a closet, because a little organization can make a big difference.

You may not be going back to school, but there is no reason you can’t have a little back to school shopping of your own. Getting a few of these items will make you feel like you are getting in on the shopping frenzy, and you will also have some fun buying yourself something new! How do you go back to school shopping as an adult?
