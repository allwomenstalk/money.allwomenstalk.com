# 7 Signs Your Spending is out of Control ...

If you are wondering if there are specific signs your spending is out of control, there are. Spending can get to be a bad habit if you aren’t careful. It can get you into so much trouble and cause so many different problems in your life. If you have any of these signs your spending is out of control, it may be time to reevaluate your spending habits and take some drastic actions to correct them.

## 1. You Have More Month than Money

If you have more month than money, it can be one of the signs your spending is out of control. It can also be a sign that you are just going through a difficult financial time so it is important to look at the whole picture here. But if you know that your spending is a part of that equation, it is time to evaluate that. How much are you spending in relation to what you are making? If it is putting your bills behind, then it is time to cut back to spending on necessities only for a while.

## 2. You Are Living on Credit

Living on credit is never a good solution. It is a sign that your finances are in dire shape. That could be because your spending is out of control. This often happens if you are spending beyond your ability to support it. Take a close look at your spending habits and see if they are part of this problem.

## 3. Shopping is Your Main Hobby

Okay, ladies. I love to shop. I mean, I really love to shop. But shopping is not my only hobby; I also love to read, spend time with my family, watch stacks of movies and hang out with my best friends. If your main hobby is shopping and you really don’t have any others, that can be a sign that your spending is out of control.

## 4. You Hide Purchases

Hiding purchases is a sign of one of two things or possibly both. There are problems in your relationship or there are problems in your spending habits or maybe both. If you have to hide purchases from your partner, it is time to think about that seriously. Is the problem that you are overspending or that your partner is overly tight with money? You should never have to hide purchases from your partner. If you are doing so, this is a problem that needs attention.

## 5. You Feel Unhappy Unless You Are Spending Money

Spending money can be fun. I think we can all agree with that statement. But if you don’t feel happy unless you are spending, there is an issue that is screaming for attention. If spending gives you a high of sorts, it is probably out of control. Try to find other things that make you feel fulfilled and happy.

## 6. You Are Ignoring Your Financial Situation

If you know that you are in dire financial straights but yet you keep right on spending, then yes, you probably have a problem. Your spending is more than likely out of control. Going out shopping when you know that your utilities are likely to be cut off is irresponsible and a cry for help. Don’t be ashamed of needing help. The real shame is not reaching out for it when you need it.

## 7. You Are Fighting over Money with Your Partner

Money fights happen in most relationships from time to time. Maybe you don’t agree about the way bills should be paid or the best investments to make. But if you are fighting over money constantly, it can be a sign that there is a problem with your spending habits. Try to see your partner’s point of view. Are they right in saying you are spending too much?

These are just a few of the many signs that you could have that your spending is out of control. Have you ever had trouble with spending too much? How did you get it back under control?
