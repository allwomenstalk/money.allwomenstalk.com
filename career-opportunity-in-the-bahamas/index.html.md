# Awesome and Unique Career Opportunities in the Bahamas ...

Looking for a **career opportunity in the Bahamas**? The history of the Bahamas begins with the earliest arrival of people to the island in the 1st millennium AD. The inhabitants were Lucayans, Arawakan speaking Taino people. The Bahamas became a colony of Britain in 1718 and around the turn of 19th century, with a little help from the government, businesspeople built hotels and established a steamship service. Tourism quickly became the main economic driver for the area. Today, people and relationships take precedence over business and strict schedules. Spending time with family and meeting and laughing with friends is essential everyday occurrences for Bahamians.

I am here to tell you about a **career opportunity in the Bahamas** at the Baha Mar resort. They are looking for a Chief Flamingo Officer. Yes, someone to run a bird daycare center! According to the job posting, the new hire will work with their chief scientist to care for the resident flock of flamingos set to arrive this spring. Duties include:

## 4. Providing Educational Tours for Resort Guests

The Flamingo Mansion is the job site. It is a huge indoor-outdoor habitat built to meet the needs of the flamingo species. The program is part of the Baha Mar Ecological, Aquatic Conservation Habitat Sanctuary. Qualified candidates should have a college degree in zoology, at least 5 years of experience working with exotic birds, extensive knowledge of biology and avian science, and excellent people skills. The application deadline for this unique career opportunity is February 28, 2018.
