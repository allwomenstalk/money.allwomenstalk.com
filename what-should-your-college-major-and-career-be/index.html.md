# What Should Your College Major and Career Be?

It's hard to decide on a college major, let alone on a career path as a whole. That's why you've probably taken a bunch of career assessment tests in the past.

However, there's now a new website that is meant to help you out even more than those tests you've taken in the past have. It's called [Find Your Calling.](https://www.findyourcalling.com/assessment)

According to [Teen Vogue,](https://www.teenvogue.com/story/website-helps-pick-college-major-career?mbid=social_twitter) all you have to do is answer six simple questions, and then state what type of degree that you're planning on pursuing. After that, it'll tell you which types of careers match your personality. It's that simple!
