# Which Career Fits Your Zodiac Sign?

Are you struggling to find a career path? Well, [Your Tango](https://www.yourtango.com/2015282067/your-perfect-career-according-to-your-zodiac-sign) might be able to help you out. According to their site, here are the best careers for your zodiac sign:

## 1. Aries

![Aries](https://img.allw.mn/content/qv/m5/w18c21dw563126fb0e238088464955_420x234.gif)

Best careers: Entrepreneur; extreme athlete; wilderness guide; CEO.

## 2. Taurus

![Taurus](https://img.allw.mn/content/if/po/a54y0rxh563126fa506e7742774262_450x300.gif)

Best careers: Architect/construction; landscaping; conservation.

## 3. Gemini

![Gemini](https://img.allw.mn/content/yd/hy/i6hrz76u563126f992e11211244050_339x264.gif)

Best careers: Marketing executive; sales/advertising; newscaster.

## 4. Cancer

![Cancer](https://img.allw.mn/content/ax/up/s2r1w92t56312703a11ca886848547_500x313.gif)

Best careers: Doctor/nurse; educational professional; realtor; chef.

## 5. Leo

![Leo](https://img.allw.mn/content/ob/zt/oltviuwu563126faa0d7b973229527_500x241.gif)

Best careers: Actor/actress; drama teacher/professor; model; entertainer; motivational speaker.

## 6. Virgo

![Virgo](https://img.allw.mn/content/sr/i7/d62dez7e563126fba2f2a087893905_500x206.gif)

Best careers: Researcher; writer/reporter; software developer; detective; nutritionist.

## 7. Libra

![Libra](https://img.allw.mn/content/pz/lm/kpxk2hm956312711cb48d633949713_500x281.gif)

Best careers: Lawyer; banker; dancer; therapist.

## 8. Scorpio

![Scorpio](https://img.allw.mn/content/wj/jm/gfxtdo8m56312708d300a838524509_500x275.gif)

Best careers: Investment banker; financial advisor; sex therapist; investigator.

## 9. Sagittarius

![Sagittarius](https://img.allw.mn/content/on/rm/ckq6dbep563126fc12215870816370_450x250.gif)

Best careers: Teacher; philosopher; travel agent; realtor.

## 10. Capricorn

![Capricorn](https://img.allw.mn/content/p7/uq/uwm4digf563127141fd6a069659027_499x255.gif)

Best careers: Business executive/manager; architect; scientist.

## 11. Aquarius

![Aquarius](https://img.allw.mn/content/t8/hw/aqccaai45631ccf688e5c913981528_500x280.gif)

![Aquarius](https://img.allw.mn/content/ad/re/jhlcsvrw5631279784b97884505974_540x236.gif)

Best careers: Humanitarian; inventor/entrepreneur; scientist.

## 12. Pisces

![Pisces](https://img.allw.mn/content/yk/r7/lv0snaoq563127053b541459916245_500x264.gif)

Best careers: Photographer; medium/psychic; film director; writer; artist.
