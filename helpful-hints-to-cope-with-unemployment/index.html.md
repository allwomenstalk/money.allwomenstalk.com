# 8 Helpful Hints to Cope with Unemployment ...

Coping with unemployment is hard, as it´s a depressing state to be in. You don´t know how long you´ll be without a job, and the seemingly endless rejections don´t make you feel any better. The longer it lasts, the more you wonder how to cope with unemployment. So if you´re one of the many coping with unemployment, here are some tips on managing the situation …

## 1. Don´t Waste Time

My first tip on how to cope with unemployment is not to waste time. As soon as you find out that you´re losing your job, start looking for another. Worrying or feeling sorry for yourself won´t achieve anything. The longer you are unemployed, the harder it is to find work, so do your best to ensure it´s a short period.

## 2. Network

It´s well known that most jobs are found through contacts, and that many are never advertised on the open market. So make as many contacts as you can. Tell everyone you meet that you´re looking for a job, and ask them to let you know if they hear of anything.

## 3. Volunteer

Volunteering is a popular way of gaining experience to improve your resume, and of occupying your time while unemployed. It may also lead to paid work, so consider volunteering if you don´t already do so. You´ll also find it an excellent way to keep busy.

## 4. Nothing Personal

It´s easy to think that you´ve lost your job because you weren´t good enough, or that you´ll never find another one. Don´t let yourself become too down – the economy is tough, and a lot of people are out of work. It doesn´t mean that you´re useless; chances are you were simply a number. So don´t take it personally.

## 5. Creative Thinking

Obviously you´ll want to get another job as soon as possible. Coping with unemployment is easier if you´re doing as much as you can to find work. Be creative in how you go about it – there may be all kinds of opportunities that you won´t find via searching through the papers, or going to an employment agency.

## 6. Don´t Give up

There´s no denying that coping with unemployment is tough, as it´s very disheartening. However, try not to abandon hope – if you give up, you´ll never find a job, as one won´t just fall in your lap! Remain optimistic, keep at it, and the opportunity you´re looking for will arise.

## 7. Seek Help

Being unemployed can be a very lonely situation. But there is help out there. Look for any source of advice on jobhunting, talk to anyone who can give you suggestions, and sign up for any courses or training that can help boost your prospects.

## 8. Expand Your Horizons

Finally, keep your options wide open, and don´t rule out any possibilities. If work comes your way, consider taking it, even if it´s not what you trained for or want to do. It´ll pay the bills while you look for something else. And keep an open mind – there may be a great job out there in a field that you´ve never considered!

Coping with unemployment isn´t easy, and it´s tempting to give up hope. Don´t despair though, as you´ll do much better if you keep positive. Making efforts will eventually pay off. So good luck with the job search! Have you any tips from your own experience on how to cope with unemployment?
