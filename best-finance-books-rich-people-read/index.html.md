# 7 Best Finance Books Rich People Read ...

If your goal is to become a rich and successful business-person, than you should definitely check out this list of the best finance books. You don’t only need money to build a financial empire; you also need knowledge. It’s extremely important to find a book that can answer all your financial questions and which will guide you and help you make the right decisions to maintain and increase your wealth. There are books that can inspire you to take up new challenges and sometimes, they can even shape your life. Here are 7 of the best finance books rich people read.

## 1. Rich Dad, Poor Dad – Robert Kiyosaki

_ **On iTunes at:** itunes.apple.com_
This is actually one of the best finance books most successful people have read. It’s an insightful book that has changed the way people look at investments. It shows the difference between a rich man and a poor man and the decisions that define each one of them. It’s one of the all-time classics in finance books, since it offers you a different view of the financial world.

## 2. The Total Money Makeover – Dave Ramsay

![The Total Money Makeover – Dave Ramsay](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t3/gk/m8eqicmg_600x772.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://books.apple.com/us/book/the-total-money-makeover-classic-edition/id666901823?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
This is another finance book you should read if you want to improve your financial life. Apparently, according to Publisher's Weekly, "The bedrock of his system is simple: work hard, pay what you owe and stay out of debt." Ramsey is well-known for his “snowball” strategy of getting out of debt which focuses on paying the small debts first “while paying only the minimum for all other debts”.

## 3. The Intelligent Investor – Benjamin Graham

![The Intelligent Investor – Benjamin Graham](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ph/ah/fm5rwam8_599x883.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://books.apple.com/us/book/the-intelligent-investor-rev-ed/id363689140?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
This book was actually written in 1949 and it’s considered to be the grandfather of investment strategy books. This book emphasizes the importance of fundamental analysis and that you should truly understand your investments if you don’t want to lose any money. It will also teach you how to keep your emotions out of your investments, so you’ll be able to reach all your financial goals.

## 4. Real Money – Jim Cramer

![Real Money – Jim Cramer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oa/nq/su37ryhl_461x700.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://books.apple.com/us/book/jim-cramers-real-money/id381499370?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
This is another finance book you should read if you want to achieve all your financial goals and learn to maintain and increase your wealth. It actually provides a very interesting analysis of the basic techniques the author advocates for buying stock. I’m sure you will enjoy this book and you might find it rather useful; especially if you’re an investor.

## 5. The Richest Man in Babylon – George Clayson

![The Richest Man in Babylon – George Clayson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d5/ae/fqpomihd_600x1025.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://books.apple.com/us/book/the-richest-man-in-babylon/id412927178?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
This book is very similar to “Rich Dad, Poor Dad” and it’s actually a collection of parables about money. The advice given in this wonderful book is extremely simple and easy to understand and for a few people, those parables and little stories will offer a new perspective on financial decisions.

## 6. The Millionaire Next Door – Thomas Stanley, William Danko

_ **On iTunes at:** itunes.apple.com_
The authors of this book have interviewed a number of millionaires in order to find a common denominator among wealthy people. They have discovered that rich people actually budget, live below their means and also, they allow their grown-up children achieve success independently. It’s very eye-opening, it sends a simple but powerful message and it’s a fun read.

## 7. Think and Grow Rich – Napoleon Hill

![Think and Grow Rich – Napoleon Hill](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o2/pa/eh4zbs2c_600x1066.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://books.apple.com/us/book/think-and-grow-rich/id554687637?amp%3Bat=11l4BD)
This is definitely a book everyone should read in order to improve their lives and their positive thinking. It was first published in 1937 and it’s one of the best “success books” out there. This is definitely one of the best self-development books of all time and it’s a must-read for anyone who wants to grow and improve their life.

There are a lot of amazing and extremely useful finance books that can help you achieve all your financial goals. I just mentioned a few in this little article but I’m sure there are many more I could add to this list. What other amazing finance books do you know?
