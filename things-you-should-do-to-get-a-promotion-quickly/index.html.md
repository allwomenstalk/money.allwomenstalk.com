# 7 Things You Should do to Get a Promotion Quickly ...

If you feel like you’re stuck in your current position and you don’t really know what you can do to climb the corporate ladder, I must tell you that there are quite a few things you should do to get a promotion quickly. Some of the highest-paid people in business use a series of methods and techniques (which I will talk about in this article) to get more money for what they do and to get promoted faster, so they’ll be able to have more authority and responsibilities. Here are a few extremely important things you should do to get a promotion quickly that will help you put your entire life and career onto the fast track and that will help you make more progress in the next couple of years:

## 1. Work for a Company That Can Give You Room to Grow

If you want to make more progress and get promoted quickly, then it’s extremely important to work for a company that can give you room to grow. Try to find a high-growth industry that will allow you to climb the corporate ladder much faster than you would do in a slow-growth industry. In my opinion, this is definitely on of the most important things you should do to get a promotion quickly.

## 2. Exceed Company Expectations

Another excellent way to get a promotion quickly is by exceeding your company’s expectations every single day. For example, if you are given a new assignment, try to always make a plan on how you can take care of that task in a shorter amount of time and how you can increase the quality of your work.

## 3. Take on Additional Responsibilities

If you can, and if you are able to find some extra time during your work day, you could take on some additional responsibilities. This way, your bosses will realize that you are over-qualified for the work you’re doing right now and that you are ready to face the challenges that come with a promotion.

## 4. Don’t Be Afraid to Share Your Ideas

If you have a better solution to one of your company’s problems, then just step forward and share it with your bosses. Don’t be afraid to say what’s on your mind, because even if your solution is not what they are looking for, at least you will show them that you have courage and that you always try to do what’s best for the company you work for.

## 5. Develop a Positive Attitude

A lot of studies show that no matter how talented or skilled you are, 85 percent of your success in work is actually determined by your attitude and your personality. It’s extremely important to develop a positive attitude because this way, you will be more likely to be promoted faster and to earn more money.

## 6. Be a Team Player

Always, and I mean always, be a team player! Employers always search for people who can obtain amazing results when working in a team environment. You do know that a workplace that fosters strong teamwork can accomplish more in a shorter period of time, so try to create a positive image and be a dependable team member.

## 7. Participate in Ongoing Learning

In order to get a promotion more quickly, try to always participate in ongoing learning. Just take some initiative and read some professional literature, attend training courses or different seminars that will help you improve your skills and become a more valuable and dependable employee to your employer. I’m sure your hard work will be noticed and you will obtain that well-deserved promotion.

It’s not easy climbing the corporate ladder; you have to be a patient team player, you need to work hard and you need to also be an ambitious self-promoter.
