# How to Make the Most of Summer Sales for Budget Savvy Girls ...

Before you go to the mall or your favorite store and take full advantage of the summer sales, I recommend you read these six golden rules for making the most of summer discounts!

## 1. Do NOT LET the DISCOUNTS DECIEVE YOU

![fashion model, shoulder, joint, dress, model,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yx/oz/z7v5959f5956df6df2b25844325689_450x1080.jpg)

No matter how affordable the prices are, nothing is cheap, even if it only takes up place in your wardrobe. Imagine, British survey found that buyers did not buy 30 billion euros worth of clothing this year!

## 2. BE a WISE INVESTOR

![clothing, dress, cocktail dress, fashion model, shoulder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f4/uh/v1usjja65956df7c65bc2195053365_564x578.jpg)

Now you already have enough clothes for this season, so consider what comes next. What could you buy now and wear in autumn and winter? Classic jackets and pants, quality sports clothes… It can all be a good investment, but, I am repeating, buy only if you like it, and not because it's on sale. Think carefully about what you really need.

## 3. DON’T BUY SOMETHING YOU WON’T WEAR and SOMETHING YOU ALREADY HAVE

![blue, fashion model, joint, cobalt blue, shoulder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h7/te/ldbrtq5r5956df897fd6b381819912_459x840.jpg)

If you buy some clothes and then go shopping again to buy something to wear with the clothes you have just bought, your entire ensemble will come to be quite expensive. It's best to buy a piece that will go well with what you already have, or buy an entire outfit in one place, if it's on sale and the total price makes good sense.

## 4. THINK if YOU HAVE SOME IMPORTANT and GLAMOROUS EVENTS to Go to Later THIS YEAR

![dress, cocktail dress, shoulder, fashion model, day dress,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qc/so/n29vdkyx5956df9912dbe828961386_500x487.jpg)

Do you expect some weddings, promotions, glamorous parties? Summer sales are a particularly good opportunity for buying more cocktail and evening dresses. Not only you will save money, but also avoid the last-minute panic for the important event.

## 5. LOOK CAREFULLY before YOU BUY the CLOTHES and EVALUATE the QUALITY

![leg, girl, thigh, abdomen, trunk,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t2/bd/qgzld0e85956dfa88b958539876262_564x704.jpg)

This is, however, a garment that has been lying in the warehouse for half a year, and there are great possibilities for the clothes to be damaged or have stains that can’t be removed. Don’t waste the money on the item with tears, smudges, stains, missing buttons, no matter what the discount is.

## 6. DON’T BUY CLOTHES THAT DON’T FIT

![dress, cocktail dress, fashion model, photo shoot, shoulder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hb/tz/fiiiztvk5956dfb7a7c0a149021653_564x922.jpg)

Now, this is every girl's classic. We've all done that. But we should never buy something that doesn’t fit us with the intention of taking it for repair, or worse, with the intention that we will lose weight soon. It's very likely we'll be throwing our money away and crowding our closet even more with items that do not fit us well, even if they came to us with an enormous discount.
