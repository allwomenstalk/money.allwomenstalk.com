# Surefire Strategies to Make the Most of Black Friday Shopping ...

Black Friday shopping is a big deal. It’s typically when you can score the best deals of the holiday season, but it can also be a bit overwhelming if you dive in without any preparation. These are 7 strategies to make the most of Black Friday shopping.

## 1. Do Your Homework

If you want to make the most of Black Friday shopping, you’ve got to do your homework. Check the sale ads beforehand. Compare prices and figure out which stores will offer you the most deals and the biggest savings. You also might want to check store policies about any questions you may have. A little prep work goes a long way.

## 2. Map It out

You’ll get the most out of your Black Friday shopping when you map out your route beforehand. You want to go to the stores that have the earliest and shortest lasting deals first. Then you want to hit up the stores where you want to be certain not to miss out on their all day savings. It’s a matter of prioritizing. You’ll get more bargains when you map out your route.

## 3. Split up

If you’re shopping with someone, it can definitely be an advantage. You can go to one store and get the deals you both want and your friend can go to another store and do the same. The divide and conquer strategy works very well. This is really the best way to approach Black Friday shopping. You don’t miss out on deals when you can split your shopping lists.

## 4. Have a Plan

It’s always best to tackle Black Friday with a plan. Think about who you need to shop for and how much you’d like to spend on them. It’s good to sit down and make a master shopping list before you begin your holiday shopping. It’s also good to think about what you want to get them rather than spending time considering different options on Black Friday. That’s valuable shopping time!

## 5. Check for Price Matching

Many stores offer price matching. What this can mean for you is that one store might be willing to honor the sale price another store is offering. This can be very helpful. It allows you to get more shopping done in one place. That’s less time that you have to spend traveling back and forth between different stores.

## 6. Wear Comfy Shoes

It’s essential to wear comfy shoes when you’re out shopping on Black Friday. The last thing you want is for aching feet to cut your shopping trip short. Wearing comfortable shoes eliminates the issue of painful feet. You’ll move faster and get more shopping done. You’ll also probably be in a much better mood than if your feet hurt.

## 7. Don’t Forget Cyber Monday

Cyber Monday is a great way to catch wonderful sales without leaving the comfort of your home. Sometimes the very same deals are offered on Cyber Monday as on Black Friday. Sometimes there are different deals altogether. With more and more people choosing to do their holiday shopping online, Cyber Monday is a great part of the best weekend of sales of the holiday season. If you’ve already finished your holiday shopping, you can use this time to treat yourself to some things you’ve been wishing for.
