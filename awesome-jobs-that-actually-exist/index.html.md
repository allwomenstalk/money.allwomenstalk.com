# 7 Awesome Jobs That Actually Exist ...

Since most daily jobs can get a bit boring and even mundane at times, here are 7 awesome jobs that actually exist. A lot of people find themselves daydreaming about the things they would like to do rather than staying at work, but for some people working might not seem like such a chore, especially if they have some of the most wonderful jobs ever. Of course, everybody has their opinion on what the perfect dream job entails, but not all of them require big fancy degrees or incredibly long hours. Here are 7 awesome jobs that actually exist:

## 1. Toy Tester

This must be one of the most awesome jobs that actually exist. This job title says that work and play are synonymous, so what more could you want from such a job? You’ll get to test products that have yet to hit the consumer market and you will not only have to ensure that those toys are fun but also that they are safe to play with.

## 2. Private Island Caretaker

Some people might say that this is the best job in the world since you will be living in an island paradise while keeping someone else’s property in top condition. You could say that this job can be a near permanent and rent-free vacation and all you need to do is to possess the skills and knowledge to tackle island life.

## 3. Chocolate Taster

This job is just about every kid’s dream, getting paid a lot to just sit around and eat a lot of candy. Yet despite the job title, this does require a bit more work since chocolate tasters must have a refined palate and they should know what subtle differences they should be aware of when they sample the chocolates.

## 4. Cruise Ship Entertainer

This job gives you the opportunity to travel around the world. You’ll get to see some of the most beautiful countries, visit ancient sites and unheard of islands and explore the open waters. You’ll get to interact with a lot of happy vacationers and your responsibility will be to make them feel even better.

## 5. Body Part Model

I must tell you that a lot of top agencies are looking for people who have beautiful features to showcase certain products. This way, your shiny long hair can be appreciated, your flawless wrists won’t go unnoticed and your wonderful fingernail beds will get some credit. Body part models can showcase all types of products and they come in all shapes and sizes.

## 6. Waterslide Tester

Yes, there really is such a job and it is just as awesome as its title implies. Tommy Lynch was one of those happy individuals who got to travel around the resorts to make sure that their pools and waterslides are up to par. He actually traveled around the world testing waterslides in Mexico, Jamaica, Egypt and Greece.

## 7. Zoologist

This is such an awesome job! You would be basically a scientist who gets to study, watch, research and care about wildlife. Zoologists are often employed by universities, zoos, museums, aquariums and wildlife reserves so you will basically be a permanent guest of the animal kingdom just by doing your job.

There are a lot of wonderful jobs out there. I just mentioned a few in this little article but I’m sure that there are many more I could add to this list. Do you know any other awesome jobs that actually exist? Please tell us about them in the comments section!
