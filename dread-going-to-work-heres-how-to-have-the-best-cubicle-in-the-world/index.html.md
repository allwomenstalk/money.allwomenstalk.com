# Dread Going to Work? Heres How to Have the Best Cubicle in the World ...

Work’s no fun when you hate your environment. Cubicles can be confining and dull. But they don’t have to be. There are lots of things you can do to give yourself the best cubicle in the world. Try some of these ideas so that you look forward to going to work.

## 1. Get Rid of the Clutter

If you want to have a cubicle that you love working from then you’ve got to get rid of the clutter. Clutter can be overwhelming. You may not even realize how much it bothered you until it’s gone. Take some time out of your workday to go through things. Trash what you can and take things home that don’t need to be at work.

## 2. Do Some Cleaning

After you’ve decluttered your cubicle, then it’s a good time to do some cleaning. Even if your office has a cleaning service, there are still probably certain spots that get missed. Check the corners and power cords for dust. Cleaning your cubicle will make it feel much better. It’s easier to be productive in a clean environment, too.

## 3. Tackle Disorganization

Are you the queen of disorganization at work? It can happen. The good news is that there are lots of organizational tools and accessories to turn your cubicle into a place where you enjoy being. It can even be fun to organize your work things. Having an organized work area has a nice side effect too; you’ll get more work done in a shorter amount of time.

## 4. Check the Lighting

Bright overhead lights fill the purpose of giving you sufficient light but they aren’t particularly helpful at giving you a pleasant environment. You might have the option of turning the overhead off and working by lamplight instead. Lamps tend to give a warmer, softer feel to a room. Softer lighting can help you to feel relaxed so that you can focus on your work. Compare these two sorts of lighting and notice the difference.

## 5. Personalize It

Every cubicle needs some personalization. Add some of your favorite photos so you can feel close to those you love while you’re working. You might want to add some small decorative accents,too. The amount of things you can add probably depends on the amount of space you have and the rules of your company. But whatever you add will lend a feeling of cheer.

## 6. Make It Comfortable

Most people spend a lot of time at their workplace. It makes sense to make it as comfortable as possible. Working at a desk can actually put you at risk for certain injuries if you aren’t careful with your posture and the placement of your hands and wrists when you type. There are many devices that can help you to have a more ergonomically friendly environment. Footstools, back support cushions and many other aids can make your cubicle a more comfortable place to work.

## 7. Make It Private

If it’s possible, you may want to take some steps to make your cubicle more private. Most people work better when they can shut off interruptions, at least to some extent. Rearrange your work area so that your back is to your coworkers if you desire privacy. There are other options that you can try, too. It’s all about what you need to be at your best for work.

These are 7 ideas to help you have a cubicle that you love. Which ones do you plan to use? I’d love to hear from you.
