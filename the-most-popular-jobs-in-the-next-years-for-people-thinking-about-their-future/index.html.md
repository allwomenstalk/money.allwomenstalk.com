# The Most Popular Jobs in the Next Years for People Thinking about Their Future ...

Are you thinking of a new job or career change in the new year and are wondering about will be the **most popular jobs in the next 10 years**? Thinking about what you want to do for the rest of your life can be intimidating. But it can also be fun and exciting! There are a ton of jobs that will be popular in the coming years, so we thought we’d fill you in and get you ahead of the game 👍 If you're stuck for ideas, maybe something in the **most popular jobs in the next 10 years** will inspire you.

## 1. Personal Brand Coaches

The importance of standing out from the crowd and creating a personal brand is already a trend we see on social media📱 Since a lot of companies hire freelance workers, it is important to do so. Being a personal brand coach to promote and develop skills in others may be a big job market in the coming future.

## 2. Freelance Professors

Almost every university has some sort of online degree program💻 which means that the growth and demand for roles as an online professor will be huge. The use of these flexible online classes by students is only expected to increase.

## 3. Urban Farmers🌽

We might think of farming as a thing in specific areas by a select few, but this is predicted to change as more and more people are trying to be eco-friendly. People see the harmful effects industrial farming can bring and want local options.

## 4. Senior Care Worker👴🏽 👵🏽

Since the baby boomer generation is only getting older, in the next decade or so it is expected that there will be a huge shift in the economy. As the population gets older, more healthcare workers will be needed to take care of them.

## 5. Smart Home Handyperson

Technology is growing at a rapid rate, and many of the things in our homes are there to make it run as efficiently as possible. But with anything, there will be ups and downs, so maintenance workers for these home tech products are going to be in demand.

## 6. Virtual Reality Experience Designers✍🏼

With virtual reality systems coming out on the market, there will be openings for the tech savvy and creative minds to design these experiences, from roller coasters to job simulations and everything in between.

## 7. 3D Printer Design Specialists

3D Printers are super cool. While they aren’t super accessible to us as a public, they do serve a lot of uses and the objects they print can be pretty amazing. There will be people hired to design and print things in the future, no doubt.

## 8. Sex Worker Coach

Believe it or not, people are predicting that robots may be used for sexual gratification in the future. While there certainly are toys and objects that can make things fun 😏 there will always be a need for human interaction. Sex coaches are still going to be in hot demand, especially to offer alternatives to growing technology.

These are just a few of the jobs that we might see take off in the near future. There is certainly a lot of predictions and excitement coming up, and we’re stoked to see what happens. Remember to always go for what your passions tell you -- you don't have to listen to anyone else! Tell us what one you think is the most exciting possible job in the comments!
