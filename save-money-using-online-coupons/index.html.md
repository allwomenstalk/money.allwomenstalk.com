# 6 Excellent Tips to Help You save More Money Using Only Online Coupons ...

Online shopping can be a boon for many who know how to seek out deep discounts and where to spend the time to get the best deals. And in today’s economic time, it is wise to save money on the products that keep you and your dear ones comfortable. Couponing is not new and has been around for ages. However, this money-saving practice has gained popularity recently, which is encouraging many people to want to know how it is done. Here are some tips to help you how to use online coupon codes smartly.

## 1. USE COUPONS on ITEMS YOU ACTUALLY WANT

Clipping coupons can be so much fun, but you should not waste your time on things you hardly use. If you get excited about purchasing something that you don’t need, it will not help you save money in the long run. There is no point in buying a brand of rice that none of your family members like to eat just because there is a profitable coupon. Similarly, it is absurd to store a six-month’s supply of excessive products as it may stop you from using that money on some other needed items. So, we suggest you take a moment to think whether you actually need that item before clipping that coupon.

## 2. USE the COUPON CODES WISELY

The best time to use a coupon is when there is a sale at the store, especially lifestyle and fashion. Do not forget to browse through the weekly flyers of your favorite stores for special sales and then check if you have a coupon for that item. If so, you are going to save more money. Many stores give great deals online. For instance Zara, Allurez, Fashionmia, Amazon and many others allow customers to use coupon codes because that way they will get more sales. One of the best fashion style stores at the moment is MalaCollective.com and just like the others, they too allow you to use a coupon codes. The prices there are already pretty awesome, but as an extra you can find some discount codes for Mala Collective that will give you up to 30% extra discount on your order. Many other online stores also offer special deals during the festive season. Just grab the offer at the right time and you may be able to double your money.

## 3. FAMILIARIZE YOURSELF with the STORE’S COUPON POLICY

Every store has its own coupon policies, that means you may or may not be able to use the same coupon in two different stores. Therefore, it is wise to thoroughly read the coupon policy on the store’s website. You can whip out their policy if any disputes occur or you have any questions regarding a coupon. So, to stay up-to-date on the store's rules and restrictions on particular coupons, make sure that have gone through each store’s coupon policy before you shop.

## 4. CHECK the COUPON’S EXPIRY DATE

Nothing can be more irritating than having a great coupon only to realize at checkout that the coupon has expired. So, it is extremely vital to check the expiration dates for the coupons before making any purchases. You can keep them in a transparent envelope in order of oldest to newest so that it is easy to find the expiry coupons.

## 5. ORGANIZE YOUR COUPONS SKILFULLY

A very important rule to using coupons is to organize them perfectly so that you can find them when needed. There are a few things you can do to keep your coupons organized. First of all, link your system to the store. You can also save your coupons in a labelled index card file or a binder where you can easily find them and check their expiry date. You can also put a sturdy photo box where you can keep your coupons safely. Or you can keep a portable pouch so that you can carry your coupons with you whenever you go to the store.

## 6. KEEP TRACK of YOUR SAVINGS

Check out whether the coupons really pay you or not. If you feel that you are not saving that much money ,even after spending so much time, you should reconsider using coupons. You can use coupon tracker tools for this purpose. The best part of such tools is that you can track how much you have already used and how much money is left to spend. With these tools, you can see the percentage of the money you saved at the store on every single shopping trip. Moreover, you can even track your rebates with the help of these tools.
