# 17 Questions to Ask 🤔 Yourself when Trying to Find Your Dream Job ...

You shouldn't feel bad if you don't know exactly what you want to do with the rest of your life. It's a huge decision to make. That's why you should really think it through. If you need some help, here are a few different questions that'll help you discover your dream job:

## 1. What Are Your Hobbies?

![person, screenshot, speech, singer,](https://img.allw.mn/content/g6/i0/jmuk3tpv58b573b760479167159670_500x282.gif)

Believe it or not, you can turn a hobby into a lifelong career. So if you love to paint or write or create websites, research the activity to find what kind of jobs out there involve them.

## 2. Do You like Working with Others?

![face, person, conversation, sense, emotion,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ok/ls/routkzq758b573b7a60dc373293203_480x270.gif)

If you prefer to work alone, then try to find a job that would allow you to have your own personal space. Otherwise, look for jobs that'll give you a chance to talk to plenty of new people.

## 3. Do You Want to Work from Home?

![person, glasses, vision care,](https://img.allw.mn/content/g9/o6/cc7wq4jm58b573b7f1ddd769642066_500x282.gif)

If you want to work in your pajamas, find a job that allows you to work from home. Or, if you'd prefer to travel, find a job that allows you to fly around the world.

## 4. How Much Money do You Need to Live Comfortably?

![How Much Money do You Need to Live Comfortably?](https://img.allw.mn/content/jx/ro/gp7tgmiq56cdc0495a29f289784645_490x230.gif)

If your dream is to live in a mansion and own a dozen cars, you need to find a job that's high-paying. Otherwise, you have more options.

## 5. What do You Have Experience Doing?

![person, glasses, professional, socialite, profession,](https://img.allw.mn/content/fq/vg/smczcysi58b573b854677298550316_500x281.gif)

Your resume is important. It'll be easier for you to get a job that involves skills that you've already mastered at past jobs.

## 6. Are You a Night Owl or an Early Bird?

[https://media.giphy.com/media/11cnF1B7QXDmXC/giphy.gif](https://media.giphy.com/media/11cnF1B7QXDmXC/giphy.gif)
If you're more active at night, then you probably don't want to teach preschool kids. However, you could always teach night classes.

## 7. What Job Did You Want when You Were Little?

![person, professional, screenshot, profession, driving,](https://img.allw.mn/content/du/to/lplqy6fs58b573ba8968f158948897_600x333.gif)

Even though you might've had some wild dreams when you were a child, they could help you decide what field to go into. For instance, if you always wanted to be a singer, you could think about going into the music business somehow.

## 8. Where do You Want to Live?

![person, selfie, interaction,](https://img.allw.mn/content/hr/gp/ksebx0l958b573ba5e846788682410_245x150.gif)

If you want to live in a small town, you can't work on Wall Street. If you want to live in a city, you can't work on a farm.

## 9. What’s the Last Thing You Want to do?

![hair, person, blond, beauty, brown hair,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ov/lg/ra92g3ej58b573ba425f7736780276_500x263.gif)

If your worst nightmare is doing customer service, you shouldn't try to get a job at a supermarket or movie theater. There, you'd be dealing with plenty of people.

## 10. Do You Want to Be Your Own Boss?

![hair, face, person, nose, hairstyle,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ak/b6/ax5c3lpm58b573bac2e39173466240_500x250.gif)

If you do, then you can start your own business. Otherwise, you can apply to a business that's already booming.

## 11. What’s Your Passion in Life?

![person, child,](https://img.allw.mn/content/x5/q3/h040nqtj58b573bb2cc2b846189998_500x300.gif)

You should follow your passion. It doesn't matter what it is. You need to go after it.

## 12. What Professions do You Admire?

![screenshot,](https://img.allw.mn/content/xb/jz/z6g4d72a58b573bc58097914447631_500x211.gif)

If you admire firefighters, you might want to become one. Why not?

## 13. What do Your Parents do?

![What do Your Parents do?](https://img.allw.mn/content/rz/mc/bri1f33b56cdc0499eb67796023441_500x281.gif)

You don't have to follow in your parents' footsteps. Of course, if they're doctors, then it'll be easier for you to become a doctor, because they can teach you all that they know.

## 14. What Jobs Have You Loved in the past?

![What Jobs Have You Loved in the past?](https://img.allw.mn/content/qu/oy/re04v9dk56cdc0492f82c083510704_480x206.gif)

If you've already had a job that you've loved, then you should try to get a similar job in the future.

## 15. What Jobs Have You Hated in the past?

![What Jobs Have You Hated in the past?](https://img.allw.mn/content/cb/wj/rlrqjzfq56cdc049305f6037929354_499x281.gif)

If you hated stocking shelves, make sure you do something completely different in the future.

## 16. Are You Willing to Work for Free at First?

![Are You Willing to Work for Free at First?](https://img.allw.mn/content/a4/q2/jwb06ddd56cdc04972afc494229629_500x294.gif)

If you can handle working for free, you should try a few different internships and see which ones you enjoy the most.

## 17. What Would Make You Happy to Wake up in the Morning?

![What Would Make You Happy to Wake up in the Morning?](https://img.allw.mn/content/j3/hq/atp1e5o456cdc049d6169690935075_500x200.gif)

You don't want to end up at a job that you hate. You need to figure out what would make you the happiest. You're not really living if you're stuck at a job you can't stand.

Hopefully these questions will help you figure out what to do with your life. Do you know what your dream job is?
