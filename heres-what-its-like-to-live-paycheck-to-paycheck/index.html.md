# Here's What It's like to Live Paycheck to Paycheck ...

Living paycheck to paycheck is so hard. I've done it for a very, very long time and just this year, I've gotten a handle of my expenses. If you are living paycheck to paycheck, you'll totally relate to the below! Trust me. The struggle is real.

## 1. Oops, Can't Buy That, Too Expensive

![Oops, Can't Buy That, Too Expensive](https://img.allw.mn/content/rb/fi/a7m7m5eq5725625482b3c030545812_500x281.gif)

Happens all the effing time.

## 2. When You Create a Spreadsheet of Your Bills

![When You Create a Spreadsheet of Your Bills](https://img.allw.mn/content/ua/ee/y69hnb9h572562551fc59862243131_500x275.gif)

… and almost die because of how many there are.

## 3. You Try to Budget

![You Try to Budget](https://img.allw.mn/content/pd/h7/zm6r39dn57256255afd92787136223_500x243.gif)

But everything is so damn expensive.

## 4. You Scrutinize Every Penny

![You Scrutinize Every Penny](https://img.allw.mn/content/fc/a1/vig2rgda5725625510e15575600846_500x282.gif)

Because, you want to make sure you aren't choosing between gas and food for the week.

## 5. Your Hair Sometimes Looks Faded …

![Your Hair Sometimes Looks Faded …](https://img.allw.mn/content/ty/xm/s5ydxzy25725625535523206322884_500x251.gif)

## 6. Sometimes, You Have to Buy the Cheapest Makeup

![Sometimes, You Have to Buy the Cheapest Makeup](https://img.allw.mn/content/tb/fl/tp76s9w057256255c4296295183081_500x316.gif)

## 7. You Get Real, Real Inventive with Home Décor

![You Get Real, Real Inventive with Home Décor](https://img.allw.mn/content/sq/on/pqvxbaph572562561e17a972663173_500x280.gif)

## 8. Vacations Consist of a Road Trip Somewhere

![Vacations Consist of a Road Trip Somewhere](https://img.allw.mn/content/po/z0/dzfz1764572562560cf19964881745_500x251.gif)

Which isn't a bad thing.

## 9. You Pay off One Credit Card at a Time

![Eyewear, Glasses, Long hair, Brown hair, Vision care,](https://img.allw.mn/content/sc/c2/c2cmv6we5cee23fb54ab8946133006_500x250.gif)

![You Love, Love Tax Time](https://img.allw.mn/content/u0/zk/d86hvupx5725625783a15515794441_540x304.gif)

## 12. You Get Really Inventive with Your Food Too

![You Get Really Inventive with Your Food Too](https://img.allw.mn/content/wb/kp/pksrltmp57256256bf800326122499_500x244.gif)

Well, you try not to waste any food and you try to use all of your leftovers.

## 13. You Have to Limit the Times You Get Takeout

![You Have to Limit the Times You Get Takeout](https://img.allw.mn/content/wj/x4/kii8nd3q5725628c4a0a5571139718_500x234.gif)

## 14. You've Already Canceled Cable – and Live off of Netflix

![You've Already Canceled Cable – and Live off of Netflix](https://img.allw.mn/content/ns/js/bj1yew2g57256257245a5211708899_480x317.gif)

## 15. New Clothes? Luxury

![New Clothes? Luxury](https://img.allw.mn/content/v8/jx/kkgdca8757256257ca8d9417644615_500x268.gif)
