# Exceptional Organization Tips to Help You with Your Bills ...

Before I started to take my bills seriously, I was paying SO much in late fees. If you are ready to get serious about your bills, take some of these organizational tips as your own. Promise, they work!

## 1. Sort All Your Unpaid Bills

![Sort All Your Unpaid Bills](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ai/a4/htiqe3ri5737b3ad9a525087174385_500x667.jpg)

Store them in one area in your home. You also should make sure that you store your bills in one place.

## 2. Bill Trackers, They Help!

![Bill Trackers, They Help!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bd/pr/euc5g3cu5737b3ae16b47507870071_500x728.jpg)

Trust me on this! Print a few out and keep track of them.

## 3. Bill Pay Checklist

![Bill Pay Checklist](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r5/m9/mjkzup285737b3aedec5f218756122_600x902.jpg)

That way you can know which bills you've paid. How easy!

## 4. Separate Your Personal Bills and Business Bills

![Separate Your Personal Bills and Business Bills](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hq/di/bewych7h5737b3afb9953085889949_600x897.jpg)

This is especially helpful if you have your own business.

## 5. Monthly Bills, Passwords – Everything Together!

![Monthly Bills, Passwords – Everything Together!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w8/cv/cx21rb6r5737b3aeb3abf489096138_600x874.jpg)
