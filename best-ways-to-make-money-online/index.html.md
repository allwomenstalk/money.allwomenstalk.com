# 8 Best Ways to Make Money Online ...

Everyone is looking for ways to earn extra cash and the Internet offers a myriad of opportunities. Not all of them are easy to do and many are too time-consuming to pay properly. But there are some quick and simple ways for anyone to earn money from their online activities and searches. You might also find your natural or learned skills will come in useful too. Here are the 8 Best Ways To Make Money Online.

## 1. Sell Goods and Products

One man’s tat is another man’s prize. So get rid of all your unwanted and unused items by selling them online on a site such as eBay or Amazon Marketplace. And if you want to increase what you earn, consider buying wholesale those products that you know are constantly in demand, then selling them on at a profit.

## 2. Become a Freelancer

If you can string a sentence together or have a flair for design, sign up as a freelance. There are opportunities in writing articles, creating web content or designing websites and potential employers are posting new jobs every minute on a vast number of websites to which you can sign up. The downside is that, initially, you won’t earn much and you’ll have to pay a cut of your earnings to the website but if you have talent and tenacity, you’ll soon carve a niche for yourself. Look out for data entry jobs, virtual assistant positions and customer service roles. E-commerce is truly global and many foreign businesses are looking for native workers in the countries in which they operate.

## 3. Write a Blog

Anyone can write a blog, on any subject, and it’s a pastime that is becoming increasingly popular. It’s also free to do as there are blogging platforms, which will host your musings. Once you have a following, pay for your own domain name and hosting and sell advertising on your blog. It may take time to be established but writing a blog can be one of the most enjoyable ways to make money online. If you are serious about blogging and really do want to generate some worthwhile income, you will find this post useful [allwomenstalk.com](https://allwomenstalk.com/ways-to-promote-your-website/)

## 4. Play Games or Poker

It may seem like a dream come true for gamers but you can earn money by playing computer games online. Multi-player online games, competing against gamers from around the globe, are challenging but if you have the skill, can be lucrative. Online poker is even tougher – you will need nerves of steel and, importantly, skill at the game, you can win big. You can also lose big so only bet what you can afford to lose.

## 5. Complete Surveys

It can be tedious and time-consuming but answering surveys is a steady way to make money. Register with one of the many websites in the field and surveys – from the likes of government departments, retailers and research groups – will be emailed to you on a regular basis. The more you complete, the more you will earn but be aware that you will be sharing a lot of data about yourself with many different organisations.

## 6. Sell Your Photographs

If you have a talent for taking photographs, upload your best to photographic websites that will pay you every time someone downloads one of your images. Obviously, the more quirky, interesting and unusual your picture is the better and the more likely it is to be downloaded. Photographic uploading is one of the fun ways to make money online where a little work can be rewarded over and over again.

## 7. Write Product Reviews

Join product review websites to offer your opinion on products and services – and get paid for your point of view. You can choose from thousands of different products, then write the review directly on to the website. You will be paid once people have read and rated your reviews so that’s a good time to enlist the help of family and friends.

## 8. Post on Messageboards

No one is likely to join a forum with no posts or discussion, so webmasters often pay people to become regular posters in one of the simplest ways to make money online. You might be asked to discuss a particular topic or join in an ongoing discussion but either way, you’ll pick up a small fee just for taking part. Shop around for messageboards that reflect your own interests – that way, posting there regularly won’t seem like a job but you’ll still get paid for doing something you enjoy.

There are plenty of ways to make money online and all you need is to spend some time finding the ones that will suit you and your skills best. With a little imagination and a good search engine, you will soon be boosting your income with these 8 Best Ways to Make Money Online.
