# Living Well Now and in the Future ...

Experiencing a good quality of life depends on many factors. Good money management, a successful career, and adopting healthy habits are just a few. Listed below are several tips for achieving your goals.

## 1. It’s Never Too Late to Advance Your Career

If you find that your current employment situation isn’t taking you on an upward climb on the food chain, you can make changes. Taking a few courses in your current profession or switching gears and starting a new career are possible. Maybe you want to go from a sales-oriented background to pursue your [interest in law](https://www.immerse.education/law-summer-school/).

## 2. Budgeting Money

There are those who seem to have a knack for creating wealth. However, a larger percentage of the workforce is clueless when it comes to managing their money. If you fall into the latter group, don’t fret. Creating a household budget will put you on track for better money management. A budget will allow you to see what you spend your money on and how much you owe out. Use this information to curb wasteful spending and learn how to save for your future and [your retirement](https://money.allwomenstalk.com/planning-your-retirement-sooner-rather-than-later/).

## 3. Living within Your Means

One of the first mistakes people make when they begin a life on their own is trying to acquire many expensive assets quickly. They purchase cars and homes and run up thousands of dollars in credit cards. Unfortunately, this reckless behavior often leads to excessive amounts of debt with no way for a fast recovery. Instead, [refrain from buying](https://www.huffpost.com/entry/the-trick-to-living-below-your-means-to-get-wealthy_b_5a1ea6ede4b0e9a1b9c7b546) large ticket items until you are able to afford them.

## 4. Benefits of an Emergency Fund

Life comes with many unexpected events. If you own an older home you may suddenly have a water leak or maybe your roof needs immediate repair. Without having [savings set aside](https://www.consumerfinance.gov/start-small-save-up/start-saving/an-essential-guide-to-building-an-emergency-fund/) for these types of emergencies, a minor repair can end up becoming a major financial setback. Take a small portion of each paycheck and set it aside in savings.

## 5. Adopting Healthy Habits

During your teen and college years you may use drugs, smoke, or consume alcohol as a way to fit in with your peers. Once you become an independent adult, it's important to limit these bad habits. The truth is, moderation is key but that lesser amount of things like wine, beer, or cigars gets lower and lower as you age. Many adults simply taper off their binges of things like alcohol or even [fast food](https://www.healthline.com/nutrition/10-similarities-between-junk-foods-and-drugs) which can be just as adverse to your body. For the most part, don’t be intimidated by stopping things cold-turkey because that never works forever, just focus on cutting back as opposed to eliminating overnight.

## 6. A Healthy Diet

Eating right is a recipe for sustained health. Fresh fruits and vegetables, lean meats, whole grains, yogurt, beans, nuts, and eggs are low in bad fats, salt, cholesterol, and sugar. A balanced diet will provide energy, focus, and health. While ordering out is convenient, most of the food choices available contain added ingredients that can add unwanted calories. An [unhealthy diet](https://health.gov/our-work/food-nutrition/2015-2020-dietary-guidelines/guidelines/chapter-2/current-eating-patterns-in-the-united-states/) will leave you feeling sluggish, unfocused, and cause you to pack on the pounds.

## 7. Retain Lean Muscle Mass and Healthy Organs

Exercise does wonders for the body, inside and out. It helps you maintain a healthy weight, form lean muscles, improves your joint function, and your internal organs’ performance. Your body is not made to sit and [remain inactive](https://www.usatoday.com/story/news/nation-now/2018/09/05/adults-worldwide-not-exercising-study-who/1199978002/). It benefits from movement. When running errands or on the job, take brisk walks to and from your destination.

## 8. Relieve Stress

Life isn’t always easy. In fact, at times it can be quite stressful. Finding ways to release stress will allow you to maintain a healthy mind and body. Yoga and [meditation](https://www.huffpost.com/entry/the-new-fun-way-to-medita_b_9471548) are two examples of productive ways to lower stress levels. Finding a hobby or enjoying a few laughs with loved ones are a few other ways to keep your mind off of your problems.

Living well now and in the future is possible. Use these tips to help you do just that!
