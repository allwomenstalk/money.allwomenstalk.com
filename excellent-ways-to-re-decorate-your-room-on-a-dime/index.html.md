# 8 Excellent Ways to Re\-Decorate Your Room on a Dime ...

Ways To Re-Decorate Your Room on a dime can come in handy if you would love a change, but don't want to break the bank! There are lots of different things you can do to switch up the feel of a room without having to spend a ton of money re-decorating. I've come up with a list of 8 of my favorite room re-decorating tips to share with you today! Keep on reading for some fabulous suggestions and bomb ideas for re-decorating your room without needing to take out a loan!

## 1. Material

Material is one of the quickest, and most inexpensive ways to re-decorate your room! You can use it to make a swag draping on the wall, a throw for the couch, or a tablecloth for your coffee table! You can even spruce up a window by creating a scarf from a piece of material. Check discount stores and clearance areas for really good deals on material!

## 2. Thrift Stores

If you are on a budget, thrift stores, flea markets, and yard sales will become your new best friend! You can find some really cool gems at bomb prices in these places. An old trunk can become a new coffee tables, a window frame can become a piece of wall art. Use your imagination, and look for unique and fun items!

## 3. Spray Paint

Oh, the wonders of spray paint! Sometimes, all it takes it changing up the color of a few items in your room to get a whole new feeling and atmosphere. And spray paint is cheap! Try doing a few "pops" of color, say a cheery yellow vase, or a robin's egg blue picture frame. Or stick to a sleek look and repaint all your chairs a chic black. Just make sure you have lots of ventilation!

## 4. Swap out

I love this idea for re-decorating your room! Get a few friends together, and have them all bring home interior decorating items they are tired of or no longer need. Then, have a swapping party! You spend zero dollars, and get lots of new and fresh items for your home. If you're not ready to part with any of your stuff yet, try this idea: Swap out items from room to room in your home. Your bedroom decor is now in the living room, and the family room decor is now in the kitchen! Just make sure you swap between appropriate rooms.

## 5. DIY Projects

Don't forget that DIY projects can sometimes add a ton of character to a room! Why not try and re-paint or re-furbish an old piece of furniture? Or learn to make your own curtains! When it comes to room re-decorating on a dime, sometime you have to put some elbow grease into it! But I promise, the results will be worth it. Your hard work will pay off!

## 6. Inexpensive Centerpieces

I love the power of large and dramatic centerpieces to completely bring a room to life! Here are a few ways to re-decorate your room using centerpieces: A large bowl a fruit, a big arrangement of wildflowers, several vases of different sizes grouped together, a glass jar filled with colored marbles, or a collage with family photos in colorful frames all grouped together.

## 7. Unique Artwork

While you are looking for ways to re-decorate your room, don't leave the walls a blank canvas! If you must splurge on one item, let it be the art. Art eases the senses, appeals to the eye, and is a great conversation starter! If you don't even have the money to spend on a piece of wall art, consider making one yourself! You could paint an abstract painting, or re-sand and paint an old and large picture frame. Then, put a pressed flower inside it for a simple, clean piece of statement art!

## 8. Slip Covers

You may not have the money to buy a new couch, but a slip cover is the fastest, most inexpensive way to swap out the look of your sofa! Buy one in a color you love, then heap on fluffy pillows and soft, warm throws. Your couch will look as cute as it is inviting, and that's a wonderful way to re-decorate your room!

These tops notch ideas and Ways To Re-decorate Your Room are splendid, and perfect for any budget, wouldn't you agree? Room re-decorating is fun, and who doesn't love a fresh new space to show off, or come home to? With these perfect suggestions for re-decorating your room, your friends will be lining up to ask you what your secret is to looking so super and still having money in the bank! Get ready to spill the beans on how to make your place look new, inviting, and gorgeous!
