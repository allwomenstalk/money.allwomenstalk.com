# 7 Tips for Turning Your Internship into a Full\-Time Job ...

Since internships are a very useful way to get exposure to companies and industries in which you would like to work, here are a few very helpful tips for turning your internship into a full-time job. Employers look for interns who are motivated, exhibit a strong work ethic, maintain a positive attitude, are loyal to that organization, are dedicated and very responsible. They want to hire someone who is dependable, trustworthy and who can work well in a team environment. Here are a few very useful tips for turning your internship into a full-time job that you should really consider:

## 1. Make Friends

One of the most important tips for turning your internship into a full-time job that you should keep in mind is to meet and greet with everyone you come across. Try to make as many friends as possible in that company because you never know when you might need a recommendation. Just work on your communication skills and always exhibit a positive and friendly attitude.

## 2. Find a Mentor

Find someone who you really admire in that company and ask them to be your mentor. Just learn from them as much as you can and allow them to assist you while you enter the field. Most professionals will love to share their expertise and their knowledge and will give you a lot of useful advice on what you can do to reach all your goals and fulfill all your dreams.

## 3. Be Professional

If you are trying to turn your internship into a full-time job, then always try to be a good professional. Show up at work on time, avoid gossip and office politics, try to maintain a positive attitude, be enthusiastic and make sure you are not taking care of your personal business while you are at work.

## 4. Do Your Research

Make sure you do your research and learn all you can about the company, their values and about objectives if you want to turn your internship into a full-time job. Just read local newspapers, professional trade journals or magazines and find out what their goals are and what you can do to help them be successful.

## 5. Ask Questions

Make sure you ask a lot of questions about everything you don’t understand in order to show your potential employers that you are motivated to learn everything you can about that industry. Internships are a good learning experience, so don’t be afraid to ask for clarifications if you don’t understand certain things.

## 6. Work with Everyone

Work with everyone around the office, even with people who are not your direct supervisors. Get to know as many of your colleagues as you can and try to interact with different people in the office. You may need some recommendations from your co-workers, so make sure you get along well with everyone. Also, show them that you are someone who can be trusted and counted on.

## 7. Ask for Feedback

If you want to be able to know if you do your job well, make sure you always ask for feedback about your performance. Be open to other people’s advice and be mature in order to take constructive criticism, so you’ll be able to grow professionally. This will show others that you are interested in the good of that company and that you are a dedicated and hard-working person.

You can turn almost any internship into a full-time job if you just pay attention to a few things and if you work hard to reach this goal. Do you know any other helpful tips for turning your internship into a full-time job? Please tell us about them in the comments section!
