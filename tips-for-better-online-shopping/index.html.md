# 5 Tips for Better Online Shopping That Every Woman Should Know ...

Online shopping can be great fun if you know the tricks of the trade. Often first-timers trying their luck online have a rather unpleasant experience. Either they are not aware of the right online shopping sites or unsure about the size or material.The fact is, online fashion shopping is a lot about instincts combined common sense. What you need is some **tips for better online shopping** to help you out. So it's time to put your shopping hats on, log onto your favorite site and follow these **tips for better online shopping** to optimize your buying experience.

## 1. PAY ATTENTION to SIZE DETAILS

This is one of the cardinal rules of online shopping. Just because you usually fit into a certain size, don't rush to buy anything that catches your fancy in that size. It is always a good idea to refer to the size chart for each product. Look for specific bust, waist and hip measurements and then choose the perfect size.

## 2. GO for KNOWN BRANDS

Let's say you fit into a specific size in Zara or Forever 21. It is always a good idea to stick to these brands when you start off with online shopping. Of course, you can experiment with more brands and different fits as you gain confidence.

## 3. PAY ATTENTION to FABRICS

An uncomfortable fabric can even make the best looking dress a bad choice. It is always important that you pay attention to the fabric and be sure it's something you're going to feel good wearing.

## 4. CHOOSE DESIGNS & PATTERNS THAT SUIT YOU

You know what cuts and fits look best on you, so look for clothing that fit those requirements when you shop online. You'll be so happy when your package arrives.

## 5. CHECK the RETURN POLICY

There are times when you might want to experiment. In that case, be sure you understand the return policy. If you see that the products have a free return policy with easy pickup and delivery services, then your shopping can be more fun and you can check out many new patterns or designs without worrying that you might have to send the item back.

Make sure you take advantage of sales and become a repeat customer too. Happy shopping!
